ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Sr. Principal Process Improvement Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Colorado Springs, CO.

Position Overview:

  

The Sr. Principal Process Improvement Analyst will join the Missile Defense Solutions (MDS) Team to work on multiple programs within the Business Unit (BU).

Responsibilities will include working with internal stakeholders to identify, prioritize and quantify improvement efforts resulting in tangible or intangible operational efficiencies. You will be responsible for primary support of the Colorado Springs location but will work liaise with and provide secondary support to the Huntsville, AL location.

Essential Functions:  

  • Work with key stakeholders within functional and program areas to identify key improvements and provide leadership, support and/or monitoring (depending on needs of project)
  • Engage in targeted activities with program teams at the request of the leadership
  • Lead/Coach/Mentor cross-functional teams to develop solutions to problems of varying complexity which require a high degree of ingenuity and creativity
  • Establish performance metrics to measure and sustain improvements, including identifying financial impacts of improvements
  • Familiarity with building, communicating and presenting business cases and/or financial metrics related to improvements
  • Demonstrated success in leading cross-department/functional teams through projects, including improvements
  • Communicate project/ improvement progress and issues through visual, verbal, and written methods to stakeholders and business leaders (including senior management) in ways that motivate decision making
  • Comfortable managing multiple projects simultaneously, requiring varying levels of support and/or leadership
  • Ability to build and maintain positive working relationships with team members, peers, and leadership when interacting on difficult systematic problems
  • Foster a collaborative culture that sustains the momentum around employee engagement and inclusion, affordability, innovation, value growth and continuous improvement.
  • Provide coaching, training and consultation in Lean, Six Sigma, Project Management, Agile or similar project/improvement methods to colleagues pursuing certification

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 10 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree and 8 years of related experience, or 14 years of related work experience in lieu of a Bachelor’s degree.
  • Ability to organize, prioritize and self-manage a variety of improvement activities
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, including to senior leadership
  • Minimum of Six-Sigma Green Belt Certification/ experience
  • Ability and willingness to travel 25% of the time.
  • Candidates must be able to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Government security clearance at the DoD Secret level


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in an operational or engineering field
  • DoD background in Space, Aerospace or Defense
  • Six-Sigma Black Belt or Lean Expert Certification
  • Proven success in leading multiple process improvement teams/activities across programs or disciplines
  • Proactive self-starter comfortable working across different areas of the business
  • Working knowledge of Agile methodology (Scrum / Kanban) applications and Tools (Atlassian JIRA / Confluence) 
  • A working knowledge of Project Management principles and familiarity with concepts such as Integrated Management Systems
  • Intermediate knowledge in:
    • Tableau and/or Power BI for data analytics
    • SharePoint
    • Azure DevOps and/or Jira
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance
