ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Senior Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Mission Defense Team (MDT) in Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

As a Systems Engineer on this team, you will support a team of engineers to define, develop, decompose, analyze, verify, and validate requirements for weapon system. In this role, you will be supporting program leadership and the customer to define and maintain the technical baseline. Ultimately, you will support the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission by ensuring teams are aware of the compliance requirements they will need to meet. 

Candidates should be highly motivated engineers who can work both independently and in team environments.

What you’ll get to do: 

The selected candidate will join MDT and will support Nuclear Surety sub-IPT in the following areas:

  • Provide systems engineering, integration, and test support to ICBM development and modification program
  • Apply the principles of engineering to design components and systems using applicable standards and specifications
  • Participate in technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for mechanical, electrical and integrated systems
  • Perform requirements analysis, develop source data for requirements, generate test requirements and prepare test reports (Nuclear Surety Evaluations)
  • Evaluate physical security designs/implementations for compliance with DoD mandated requirements
  • Work with sub-contractors to ensure designs/artifacts are compliant with AF and DoD criteria for nuclear weapons systems
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Interface with AF customer

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) discipline from an ABET accredited university
  • US Citizen with an active DoD Secret Clearance adjudicated within the last 6 years
  • Minimum of 9 years of engineering experience with a bachelor’s in science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with a PHD
  • Minimum 3 years of experience with requirements management

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • BS degree in Electrical or Software discipline
  • Active DoD TS clearance with a background investigation close date within the past 6 years 
  • General knowledge of multiple engineering disciplines: Safety, Nuclear Hardness, Nuclear Surety, Material Science, Environmental, Parts and Logistics, etc
  • Failure Modes, Effects, and Criticality Analysis experience
  • Experience working with Nuclear Surety Requirements (e.g. AFI 63-125, AFMAN 91-118/91-119/31-108, DODM 5210.41, AFI 91-107)
  • Experience exporting documentation out of a Model-based environment.
  • Experience with Agile Development
  • Experience with any of the following tools and languages: CORE, DOORs, Rhapsody, Cameo, ModelCenter, MagicDraw, SysML
  • Experience in Systems Engineering and any of the following areas:
    • Requirements Management: MBSE, Functional Block Diagrams, Specification Trees
    • Systems and end item requirements validation and verification
    • Design: Systems Design, Systems Architecture, Systems Integration, N2 Analysis, Trade Studies
