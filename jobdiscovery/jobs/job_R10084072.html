ACME Engineering Defense Systems in Ridgecrest, CA is currently seeking an Assembler 3 to join our team.

Reporting to the Production Manager, the Assembler 3 will perform a variety of tasks ranging from repetitive to non-repetitive production assembly operations on electronic and/or mechanical assemblies and subassemblies. Works from diagrams, drawings, procedures and parts lists making early-stage subassemblies using hand and/or power tools. Perform continuity checks on work in process and completed assemblies. May disassemble, modify, rework, reassemble, and test production or prototype assemblies and subassemblies according to specifications provided by a Cognizant Engineer.

The chosen candidate will receive in-house workmanship training and certification training. This position may perform actions in Manufacturing areas including Mechanical Assembly, Circuit Card Assembly, Microelectronics Production, and Electronic Component Assembly depending on the business need or workload capacity.

This position is a great fit for someone who enjoys working in a team environment, can perform tasks under a microscope on very small assemblies; has a high level of attention to detail and quality. The chosen candidate must be able to multi-task and flex between multiple assignments

Basic Qualifications: 

  • High school diploma or GED equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or Assembler experience.
  • Production assembly operations on electronic and/or mechanical assemblies and subassemblies such as modules, boards, panels, drawers, frames, and cables. Must be able to lift up to 35lbs.
  • Able to perform a two-person lift in accordance with MIL-STD-1472.
  • Candidate must be willing to work extended hours occasionally to meet deadlines. US citizenship required. Ability to obtain Secret Security Clearance
