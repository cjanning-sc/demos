Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (Roy, UT)
R10081068
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering is seeking a Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (Level 2). This position will be located in Roy, Utah. The qualified candidate will become part of the ACME Engineering’s Strategic Space Systems Division, Global Supply Chain team. Responsible for supporting Procurement commodity teams with various supply chain related tasks.  

Responsibilities:

  • Interface with Suppliers for Purchase Order delivery status and oversight that includes but is not limited to, sending Weekly Open Order Report to Suppliers, maintain current Purchase Order delivery dates, provide status to other functions for Program visibility, request and track part milestone schedule and closeout any administrative Purchase Order actions.
  • Conduct and lead Weekly/Bi-Weekly status meetings with Suppliers and other Global Supply Chain functions.
  • Interface and collaborate with Buyer, Programs and other organizations within and outside of Global Supply Chain
  • Ability to plan schedule and arrange activities to accomplish objectives
  • Person should possess outstanding written and verbal communication skills and be extremely organized

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree and 3 years of relevant experience in supply chain management, logistics, manufacturing/project/business management, or information systems; 1 year with a Masters.  Or, in lieu of a degree, 7+ years of experience in supply chain management, logistics, manufacturing/project/business management, or information systems
  • Demonstrated leadership, critical thinking and innovative behaviors in past projects, roles or experiences
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office including Word, PowerPoint, and Excel.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Professional experience in a regulated industry; to include Aerospace & Defense, Insurance, Financial Services, Telecommunications
  • Experience with FAR/DFAR
  • Experience with ERP systems, preferably SAP
