ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal/Sr Principal Cyber Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Huntsville, AL.

Position Overview:

The Principal Cyber Engineer will provide support for the MASC-F Contract in Huntsville, AL. The selected candidate will apply an interdisciplinary, collaborative approach to plan, design, develop, validate and verify cyber solutions across the lifecycle. They will conduct cyber risk assessment activities including threat modeling, vulnerability analysis and analysis of mitigation solutions, and will also maintain the cyber infrastructure for the classified and unclassified development environments. 

Essential Functions:  

  • Provide cyber security engineering support for the development team
  • Support securing systems so they meet customer security controls
  • Support security testing and verification activities (dry runs and formal government testing)
  • Support the mitigation of findings from security testing

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

This requisition may be filled at a higher level based on qualifications:

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Cyber Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 5 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree and 3 years of related experience, or 0 years of experience with a PhD.
  • Operating System Experience and Knowledge (Linux, Windows and/or Network IOS)
  • Scripting or software language experience
  • Vulnerability scanning experience and knowledge
  • A current active U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance or higher is required to start.

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Cyber Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 9 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree and 7 years of related experience, or 4 years of experience with a PhD.
  • Operating System Experience and Knowledge (Linux, Windows and/or Network IOS)
  • Scripting or software language experience
  • Vulnerability scanning experience and knowledge
  • A current active U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance or higher is required to start.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • NIST 800.53 Experience or knowledge
  • Experience or knowledge of verifying and applying DISA STIGs
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Logical reasoning and troubleshooting experience
