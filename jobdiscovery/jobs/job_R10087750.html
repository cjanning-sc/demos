U.S. European Command Corporate Lead Executive
R10087750
Not Applicable (United States of America)

ACME Engineering is seeking a new Corporate Lead Executive (CLE) to oversee government relations with European-based DoD operational forces, with a clear focus on forces located on the European continent. Reporting to the Vice President of Air Force Programs, the CLE will be responsible for establishing and maintaining effective U.S. customer relationships across EUCOM and the service components to foster an in-depth understanding of multi-service military requirements of the European Theater. This individual will orchestrate and coordinate high level customer interface with all military services and other customer organizations designated by the Vice President. This engagement will be accomplished in collaboration with other corporate and sector teams through internal cross-sector and external strategy development and outreach. The candidate selected for this opportunity will be required to work on-site and full-time in Ramstein or Stuttgart, Germany.

Responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Coordinate, integrate, and synchronize corporate and sector customer engagements and campaign strategies, and facilitate the development of new business opportunities within customer markets.

  • Support corporate and sector teams in the design, construction, and execution of customer analysis plans, strategic customer approach plans, and customer contact plans.

  • Provide inputs for the development of company priority programs.

  • Maintain awareness of significant policy, program, budget, and legislative initiatives effecting corporate goals.

  • Travel will be required; this will primarily be domestic, including occasional visits to ACME Engineering's Corporate Headquarters in Falls Church, VA.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree with a minimum of 20 (with Bachelor's) or 18 (with Master's) years of DoD service and associated operational experience, including senior level leadership responsibilities and management experience.

  • Current DoD Top Secret/SCI security clearance; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • The selected candidate will be required to obtain a Special Access Program (SAP/SAR) clearance within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company, and also by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining a SAP/SAR clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • The ability to analyze a broad range of issues and exercise strategic thinking to best influence senior DoD civilian and military decision-makers.

  • A high level of confidence, integrity, and a hands on approach to problem solving as well as a professional executive presence for interacting with and influencing DoD decision makers, senior management, and executives internal and external to the company.

  • Experience with DoD budget, acquisition, and requirements processes.

  • Strong judgment, decision-making skills, flexibility, agility, and political acumen.

  • Unswerving commitment and the ability to work cooperatively in a team environment.

  • Excellent organizational and verbal and written communications skills, as well as project management, analytical, planning, presentation, and human relations skills.

  • The ability to travel - this will primarily be domestic, including occasional visits to our Corporate Headquarters in Falls Church, VA.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Retired Flag/General Officer or Senior Executive.
