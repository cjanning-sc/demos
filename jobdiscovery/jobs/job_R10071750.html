ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Information Technology Lead Manager 2 to join its team of diverse individuals. What you will get to do:

The selected individual will perform as an IT leader and product owner responsible for agile execution of IT sustainment and enhancement activities. The engineer/leader selected for this position will:

  • Be responsible for leading a team of IT professionals
  • Have a strong understanding of Agile principles
  • Have experience working on an Agile team
  • Provide coaching, technical direction, and facilitation of the on-site team
  • Lead scrum meetings, technical backlog reviews and software customer engagements
  • Provide support to the DevSecOps Engineers is guiding activities and ensuring the priority items are being addressed
  • Perform/support rapid software defect root cause analysis and problem resolution
  • Provide highly innovative solutions to unusually complex technical problems
  • Position will require up to 25% travel
