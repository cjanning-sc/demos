Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

The Space Systems Engineering and Integration Organization in SSSD has several openings for Space Systems Engineers to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. These positions will be located in the Space Park Center of Excellence to include Redondo Beach, Manhattan Beach and El Segundo in Southern California.

Multiple roles are available throughout the product development life-cycle of our programs with emphasis in one of the following disciplines:

  • Space Segment/Space Vehicle Systems Engineering, Design, and Architecture Development

  • Design Integration

  • Mass Properties

  • Alignments/ Sensor  Analysis/ Sensor Accommodations

  • Payload Accommodation and Integration

  • Fault Management

  • Testbeds/Vehicle Simulators

  • Launch Systems Integration

  • Requirements and Verification

  • Specification Development

  • Interface Definition

  • Functional Analysis

  • Configuration Data Management

  • Related Processes and Tools Development

Available positions require that the candidate have a strong understanding of one or more of the skills described. Project roles span all phases from new business acquisition, and IRAD/ technology development to established programs.

More specifically, a Systems Engineer is needed to manage the Space Vehicle-Level mass properties for the Ruby program.

-Current SCI clearance
