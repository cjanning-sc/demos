ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Lab Execution Manager 2 to join its team of diverse professionals in support of the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This leadership role is located in Roy, Utah. Learn more about the Sentinel (GBSD) program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

The System Test Integrated Product Team (IPT) is seeking a manager to oversee integration and checkout efforts for the system test lines in the Integrated Test Facility.

Job duties include:

  • Synthesize customer contractual needs and requirements into system test solutions that acknowledges technical, schedule and cost constraints.
  • Develop and direct preparation and execution of comprehensive test, test plans, procedures and schedules for completing systems.
  • Coordinating integrated testing activities working in a dynamic people-focused environment
  • Review and evaluate test requirements to ensure completeness and compliance of the test program.
  • Perform technical analysis of complete systems and prepare comprehensive system level evaluations.
  • Direct oversight of integration and test teams
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to ensure requirement sell-off
  • Responsible for product integration, regression, and verification/validation testing
  • Interface with US Government customers
