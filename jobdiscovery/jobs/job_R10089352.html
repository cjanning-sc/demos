ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) is seeking either a Principal Program Cost Schedule Control Analyst or a Senior Principal Program Cost Schedule Control Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. As part of a Sector level Home Room team, the selected candidate will have the ability to help shape our programsâ€™ ability to meet challenging customer compliance requirements. Tasking will include guiding programs with interpretation and execution of Earned Value compliance, process and procedure guidance and training, subcontractor compliance, and resolution of program execution issues across the NGAS suite of programs. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Earned Value Management, Planning, Program Control, and subcontractor surveillance. This position will be located on-site in Melbourne, Fl.

Essential Functions:

  • Serve as the EVM focal point between technical, cost, schedule, and program management teams
  • Build partnerships with program personnel to support program execution while maintaining compliant EVM implementation in accordance with EIA-748 and corporate command media
  • Develop and deliver Program Execution, EVM, Agile, and Best Practice training to build a shared understanding and increased capability of compliance and best practices to support program execution
  • Identify and implement EVM process & procedure updates and improvements
  • Monthly review of DCMA Earned Value Compliance Metrics (DECM) for independent analysis of program risk areas, reporting of issues to senior management, and supporting program execution team in finding suitable resolutions
  • Review, analysis and corrective action assessment on common cost / schedule management data non-compliance issues related to IPMR, IMPDAR, CFSR, Estimate At Completion (EAC), Variance Analysis Reports (VAR), reconciliation logs, etc
  • Facilitate Variance Analysis Report (VAR) and Estimate at Complete (EAC) development and approval
  • Provide guidance, oversight, and approval of program baseline implementation and changes
  • Lead and participate in both internal and subcontractor EVMS surveillance
  • Support Defense Contract Management Agency (DCMA) reviews/audits
  • Familiarity with Earned Value Management principles, practical knowledge of EIA-748 standards, experience with Customer, Internal, and DCMA Earned Value surveillance activities
  • Understanding of common cost management tools such as MPM and / or COBRA
  • Understanding of common scheduling tools such as MS Project, Primavera, or Open Plan
