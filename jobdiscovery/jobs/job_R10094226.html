ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Network Engineer – VM Ware to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Schriever Space Force Base, Colorado Springs, CO.

Position Overview:

The ideal candidate will have a rich network engineering background as well as experience implementing virtualization concepts in to network architecture. The position will support virtualization buildout and will integrate with various network support teams that focus on the C2BMC operational environment.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • Engineer with hands-on experience and knowledge of virtualization design, development, testing methodologies; experience with VMware (vCenter), ESX servers, NetApp NAS/SAN, Windows servers and SQL servers.
  • A solid understanding of the OSI Model, TCP/IP, routing protocols, network traffic analysis, diagnostic and troubleshooting experience
  • Microsoft Windows server experience with multi-user applications
  • Redhat server experience with multi-user applications
  • VMware server installation, configuration, management performance monitoring and tuning of virtual machines ESXi servers
  • Virtual machine troubleshooting and performance issues
  • Experience creating VM deployment templates and automated deployments
  • Understanding of standard DOD security practices such as, STIG compliance and system accreditation implementation efforts.
  • Thorough understanding of Network Attached Storage, specifically NetApp FAS series
  • Storage Area Network experience with high availability and performance for virtualization
  • Must have experience using the Agile Framework or experience applying Agile fundamentals in engineer-focused work

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Candidates with prior C2BMC system experience
  • Knowledge, skill sets or experience using protocol analyzers/sniffers (such as Wireshark)
  • Authentication & encryption methods/capabilities (ACS, Active Directory, GEM-X), Virtualization technologies such a VMware vCenter and vSphere
