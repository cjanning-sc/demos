ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Manager of Manufacturing Equipment Maintenance 2 to join our team in Sunnyvale, CA.

What You'll get to Do:

This individual will supervise employees who install, maintain, and repair industrial processing and manufacturing equipment. This position will coordinate with manufacturing personnel, manufacturing engineering and management to ensure that manufacturing equipment is maintained and in ready status. The successful candidate will ensure that machine and equipment metrics achieve a high level of performance, communicate readiness, drive execution of repair and engage a highly skilled team to customer satisfaction. Responsibilities include planning, designing and overseeing the reconfiguration, maintenance, and alteration of equipment, machinery, buildings, structures, and other facilities as required.


Other responsibilities include the following:

  • Lead a team of skilled technicians and electricians to drive machine and equipment performance.
  • Support a Predictive and Preventive Maintenance Program for machine tools and equipment.
  • Ensure the implementation and accuracy of the Computer Maintenance Management System.
  • Lead and Coordinate Root Cause and Corrective Action of machine and equipment failures.
  • Communicate daily status of machine downtime and repair activities.
  • Drive initiatives that lead to increased machine uptime performance.
