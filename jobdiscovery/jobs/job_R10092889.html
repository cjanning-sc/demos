ACME Engineering’s Aeronautics Systems sector communications team is hiring a Senior Principal Communications Representative who will work as a strategic advisor to the Research and Advanced Design (R&AD) directorate while also supporting the execution of the sector’s communications strategy. The candidate will lead all internal and external communications for R&AD, based in Redondo Beach, CA.

Using an objectives-based approach, the candidate will support the formulation of compelling stories to deliver measurable results. This position reports to the Air Dominance and R&AD division communications manager but will initially work closely with the communications director to formulate the communications plan for R&AD while developing a review process on execution of the sector-wide strategy.

The candidate must be a confident strategic advisor, has experience leading teams in a matrix organization, thrives on the challenge of informing and inspiring employees around business priorities and culture, and constantly seeks to improve through disciplined measurement, analysis, and adjustment.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, Journalism, English, Marketing or a related field and a minimum of 10 years of experience, or a Master's degree in prior fields and 8 years of experience.

  • Experience as a communications advisor and leader.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, strong judgement, problem solving and organizational skills, and superb attention to detail.

  • Willingness and ability to receive Department of Defense security clearance.

  • Proficient in Associated Press Style.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite (Word, SharePoint, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Communications and leadership experience in the defense industry preferred.

  • Department of Defense Secret Clearance.
