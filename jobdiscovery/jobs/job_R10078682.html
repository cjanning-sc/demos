ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking an experienced Principal Engineer Systems/Sr. Principal Engineer Systems to serve on the Strike program as part of the Systems Engineering Integration & Test team in our Chandler, AZ office.
 

Role Description & Responsibilities:

·       The candidate should possess strong technical problem solving, written and verbal communication skills to generate and document mission launch vehicle requirements, perform vehicle performance assessments and participate in mission verification/data review activities.

·       Regular interface with program management, engineering staff and external customers is expected.

·       The candidate should be willing to occasionally work during odd hours (nights/weekends) and occasionally longer than normal (>40 hrs/wk) to meet deadlines.

·       The candidate should possess a strong resolve to drive tasks to completion autonomously and possess demonstrated mentoring/training skills.

·       Provides data for and assists in risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes.

·       Supports mission design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, and failure/anomaly resolution.

·       Prepares and presents materials for at major mission milestone meetings.

·       Manages cost accounts for mission scope of work

This position may be offered at the Principal Engineer Systems Level 3 or Sr. Principal Engineer Systems Level 4.

Basic Qualifications: 

If offered at the Principal Engineer Systems Level 3 requires:

  • BS Engineering degree plus 5 years of experience; Masters degree plus 3 years of experience; PhD with 0 years of experience.

 If offered at the Sr. Principal Engineer Systems Level 4 requires:

  • BS Engineering degree plus 9 years of experience; Masters degree plus 7 years of experience; PhD with 4 years of experience.

  • Candidate must have a minimum of 5 years of related work experience.

  • U.S. Citizenship is required

  • Candidate must have a DoD Secret level security clearance and may be required to obtain a Top Secret level security clearance

  • Ability to travel is required (<25%)

 

Preferred Qualifications: 

·       A strong experienced-based and academic foundation in Systems Engineering fundamentals is desired.

·       Weapons system or RF experience

·       Experience with external customer interfacing (meetings, presentation, negotiation)

·       Requirements Management databases (DOORS) experience

·       System testing and launch operations experience

·       Previous Cost Account Management experience

·       Experience with Agile, Scrum, and/or Jira
