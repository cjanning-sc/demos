ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector, Networked Information Solutions Division (NISD), Growth and Mission Solutions is seeking a Business Development Director 2. In this exciting and challenging role, the selected individual will lead forward-looking customer engagement efforts with the Department of the Air Force. The primary objective of this role is to create and shape business opportunities that drive growth for the division. The successful candidate will bring a set of skills and experience that support the breadth of NISD capabilities in sensing, connectivity, processing, autonomy, and non-kinetic effects.  This individual will connect division strategy to business development priorities, messaging, and execution.

Acceptable locations for this role are: Northern Virginia, Baltimore, MD, and San Diego, CA. This position reports to the NISD Vice President, Growth & Mission Solutions.

Job Description

Roles and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Providing thought leadership in the successful implementation of the division’s connectivity, processing, and intelligence strategy
  • Lead business development activities to define new opportunities that will achieve growth goals for the divisions
  • Develop engagement and growth strategies for Air Force and Space Force customers based on individual and integrated capabilities from the NISD portfolio
  • Work effectively across sectors, divisions, business units, and functional teams to build a strong opportunity pipeline
  • Drive long-term transformational and integrated growth opportunities using a portfolio approach
  • Engage with the complete stakeholder community including COCOMs, MAJCOMs, OSD, Joint Staff, Congressional members and staff, thinktanks, FFRDCs, UARCs, requirements writers, and program offices
  • Coordinate with business development teams inside business units in a collaborative manner that achieves long-term growth objectives while continuing to grow the existing business
  • Leverage technical, analytical, and operational expertise from across the division in support of impactful growth opportunities
  • Support capture and proposal activities with strategies that result in high capture rates that support growth objectives
  • Use customer relationship management (CRM) tools such as Salesforce while following the process standards of the ACME Engineering Business Acquisition Process (BAP)

Basic Qualifications (Dir. 1)

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 15+ years of work experience or Master’s Degree and 10+ years
  • Experience with Air Force acquisition and operations
  • Hands-on experience in leading a high-performing team with a record of success
  • Effective communication skills with the ability to inform, influence, negotiate, and problem solve
  • Project management experience
  • U.S. Citizen with active TS clearance
  • Willingness to travel up to 25%, depending on location

Preferred Qualifications

  • Understanding of Joint All-Domain Command and Control (JADC2) and other emerging concepts
  • Experience with DoD/IC programs in connectivity, processing, and intelligence
  • Active TS/SCI clearance
