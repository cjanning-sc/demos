ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Designs, develops, documents, tests and debugs hard real-time embedded software containing logical and mathematical solutions.
  • Conducts multidisciplinary research and collaborates with equipment designers and/or hardware engineers in the planning, design, development, and utilization of embedded systems for safety and mission critical software.
  • Must be able to work and collaborate with a team of software engineers in an Agile environment to meet critical milestones. 
  • Ensures program mandated software standards and compliance criteria are met.
  • Understands and can develop in a continuous integration tool chain meeting technical and compliance requirements.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

Job Qualifications: 

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • 9 Years of experience with Bachelors degree, 4 additional years of experience may be used in lieu of a degree; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD
  • Active DoD Secret clearance with a reinvestigation date occurring in the last 5 years.
  • US Citizenship and ability to obtain Special Program Access (SAP)

Experience with C++ and object oriented design

Knowledge of targeting a real time operating system

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Holds a current in-scope DoD or DoD SAP clearance
  • Experience developing software for a hard real time system and rate monotonic scheduling
  • Experience with developing software for hardware devices
  • Experience in the implementation of GNC algorithms
  • Experience developing in an Agile environment
  • Experience with nuclear surety requirements
  • Experience developing missile flight systems software
  • Experience with DO-178C compliance
