ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Provisioning Logistics Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Sentinel program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Perform logistics analysis and maintenance planning of the Sentinel platform.
  • Review, analyze, prepare, and modify provisioning data.
  • Documentation (PTD) and related data in accordance with ACME Engineering procedures.
  • Use provisioning data to identify, select, catalog and determine support material requirements, and in the development of Provisioning Parts List and design Change Notifications.
  • Provide support for modifications, reviews, and updates to any of the above existing lists.
  • Attend and support provisioning meetings and conferences and perform other functions as directed.
  • Demonstrate excellent interpersonal skills, communications skills, be self-motivated, understand and exhibit a strong team work ethic and approach.
  • Work closely with other IPT’s (Integrated Product Teams), Design Engineering IPT’s, Configuration Management, Systems Engineering, and Technical Publication Writing teams.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either as Provisioning Logistics Engineer level or Principal Provisioning Logistics Engineer level.

  • Provisioning experience with MIL-HDBK 1388-2B and GEIA-STD-0007
  • Level 2; 2 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD. 4 years of additional experience in lieu of education
  • Level 3; 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD. 4 years of additional experience in lieu of education
  • Current DoD Secret clearance
  • Must be knowledgeable in Microsoft Office Suite - Project, PowerPoint, Excel, Word
