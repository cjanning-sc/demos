ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for either an Associate Software Engineer or Software Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida.

We offer flexible work arrangements, great learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Develop, modify, and maintain customized or standardized embedded Mission Systems applications using software engineering best practices and standards.
  • Participate in the full life-cycle of software development, to include requirements development, modeling and design, application development, unit to CSCI testing, integration, formal system testing, release, installation, and maintenance.

This position can be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.  This requisition may be filled at either an Associate Software Engineer level or Software Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for Associate Software Engineer:

  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree.
  • Experience developing software with C/C++, Java, or Python
  • This position requires the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Clearance as well as Program Special Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business needs

Basic Qualifications for Software Engineer:

  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree and 2-years of experience in software engineering OR a Master’s degree.
  • Experience developing software with C/C++, Java, or Python
  • This position requires the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Clearance as well as Program Special Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business needs

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience developing software for embedded systems
  • Experience integrating and debugging software on hardware
  • Experience working within an Agile environment
  • Experience with software development tools such as Eclipse, Git, Rhapsody, DOORS, CMake, VectorCAST. Atlassian Tools (Jira, Bitbucket, Confluence, Crucible, Bamboo)
  • Familiarity with MIL-STD-1553
  • Knowledge of Linux systems and basic commands
  • Active DoD Secret (or higher) clearance (with a background investigation within the past 6 years or enrolled into Continuous Evaluation)
  • Program Special Access
