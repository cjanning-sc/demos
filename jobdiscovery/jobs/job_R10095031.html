Classified Area (Special Access Program) Integration Manager (Manager Programs 2)
R10095031
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Classified Area Special Access Program (SAP) Integration Manager (Manager of Programs 2) will lead classified area initiatives and operations for multiple designated Launch Vehicles Programs in Chandler, AZ. This role will report to the Program Integration Organization supporting classified, Programs, including SAP.  The successful candidate will influence the direction of the classified area activities to meet the company’s strategic objectives, and balance programmatic needs to deliver results in a classified work environment. This person will be responsible to manage complex problems, projects, and have functional responsibilities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Advises on SAP and SAPF operations.
  • Serves as consultant to management and customers on closed area policies, plans, and objectives.
  • Engages and align with Customer requirements for classified operations, especially SAP operations.
  • Drives operational efficiencies that improve Closed Area program operations
  • Integrates between Program Management, Program Customer, Cybersecurity, Industrial Security and classified IT.
  • Manages program relationships to ensure all security requirements are met and in compliance.
  • Manages and delivers any required deliverables pertaining to the secure area or program requirements.
  • Develops and drives solutions to complex problems consistent with organization objectives.
  • Manages Projects with cross-functional teams to meet closed area requirements including facilities, security, and hardware/ software needs.
  • Ensures successful execution of Closed Area operations.
  • Clearly presents technical approaches and findings for initiatives.

Qualifications:

Basic Qualifications

  • Active Secret clearance in-scope (granted or renewed in the last 6 years).
  • Must have bachelor’s degree and minimum of 9 years of related professional experience to include a background in Program Management or related military experience; Master’s degree and 6 years related experience
  • Proven experience on Special Access Programs and SAP Facilities
  • Proven understanding and experience of program life cycles and program management fundamentals
  • Proven experience with Customer Engagement
  • Proven experience successfully managing technical project requirements
  • Proven Functional Management experience
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite
  • Must be able to perform occasional lifting up to 10 lbs

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active Top Secret clearance adjudicated within the last 5 years
  • Experience in Leadership roles within the Aerospace and Defense industry, or within the military.
  • Experience leading IT projects
  • Experience in cybersecurity
  • Project Management Professional (PMP) certified
