Essential Functions
 

•    Responsible for overall safety of operations and shift employees.

•    Responsible for productivity, policy and procedure compliance, operational safety, environmental protection, labor management, and cost management.  
•    Must determine staffing on each shift and adjust based upon priority and availability.
•    Responsible for employee training.  
•    Responsible for procedure review and assuring changes in process are done properly and in a timely fashion. 
•    Responsible for implementation of new systems and procedures.  
•    Required to interact with engineering and maintenance on a routine basis.  
•    Responsible for determining repair needs of the operations and assuring maintenance work is done. 
•    Responsible for assuring all tools and materials necessary are available to employees.

•    Ability to safely operate a motor vehicle.
•    Ability to operate a computer.
