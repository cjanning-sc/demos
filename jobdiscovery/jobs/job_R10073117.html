ACME Engineering is seeking a Machinist to work in our Mechanical Fabrication shop to support various programs within the Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance division. Located in McClellan / Sacramento location, this person will be performing a variety of machining and fabricating tasks, using both manual and CNC machines, making replacement as well as new parts.
Candidate will perform a variety of machining and fabrication operations using manual machines such as; lathes, milling machines, drill press, band saw, shears, metal punch. Must be able to interpret blueprints, sketches and engineering specifications to determine fabrication methodology. Setup and operate machining equipment including various hand tools to determine sequence of operations, making standard shop computations relating to dimensions of work, tooling, feeds and speeds, measures, marks and scribes dimensions and reference points to lay out stock for machining. Will be responsible for standard equipment maintenance and working with in house maintenance personnel or third party vendors as needed to keep equipment up and running; maintain machine tooling and related consumables; perform a variety of repetitive and non-repetitive mechanical assembly tasks; disassemble, modify, rework and reassemble mechanical assemblies; work with and be responsible for the storage and use of HAZMAT materials. Maintain high ethical standards for conduct, and must work collaboratively with others.

Basic Qualifications:
• High School diploma or equivalent, plus 2+ years of related experience
• Ability to interpret blueprints, sketches and engineering specifications
• Ability to set up and operate a variety of machine tools, such as; milling machines, lathes, drill press and band saw.
• Experience managing multiple projects
• Experience reporting job status

Preferred Qualifications:
• Must have good communication and collaboration skills
• General knowledge and understanding of CAD/CAM program: PRO-E or SolidWorks
• Strong ability to problem solve
• Ability to manage multiple jobs with minimum supervision, establish priorities, and report job status in a timely manner
• Must be mechanically inclined and pay close attention to detail
• Must have a high degree of patience, adapt to change easily, and have a high level of motivation to complete tasks promptly
• AA degree
• Ability to prioritize projects
