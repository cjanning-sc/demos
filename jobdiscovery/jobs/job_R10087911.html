The Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division of ACME Engineering's Space Systems sector seeks team members who want to solve interesting, complex problems which protect our nation. We are a trusted mission payload and ground system provider with extensive experience providing end-to-end mission solutions from concept through operations and support. The team you will support is located at our Space Park campus in Redondo Beach, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

As a Sr. Principal Systems Engineer on this team, you will support a team of engineers to define, develop, decompose, analyze, verify, and validate requirements for the ground segment of a satellite system. In this role, you will be interacting with the customer and leadership to define and maintain the technical baseline. Ultimately, it is your responsibility to drive the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission, ensuring everything is synchronized.

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Ground Systems Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM with 9 years of related experience, or a Master’s degree in STEM with 7 years of related experience
  • Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes
  • Active DoD Top Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access or active SCI access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile development
  • Effectively communicate and present to project and program management, other technical managers, and customers
  • Direct experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database
  • Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering languages (SysML/UML) and concepts
  • Excellent technical writing and team building skills
