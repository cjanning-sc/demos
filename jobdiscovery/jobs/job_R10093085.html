ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Sr. Principal Software Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Huntsville, AL.

Position Overview:

  

Sr. Principal Software Engineer will support the Ground-Based Midcourse Defense (GMD) program. You will be responsible for performing all aspects of software integration testing including but not limited to:

  • Ensuring generation of build product from software version-controlled baseline
  • Evaluate test execution results
  • Documenting results and product defects in established tools
  • Collaborate with software teams to understand expected behavior
  • Develop procedures to support testing of program software
  • Execution of program software in test labs
  • Setup and document system hardware and software configurations
  • Perform troubleshooting and resolution of hardware and/or software configuration issues in test labs
  • Create and/or update program related documentation

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 9 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline and 7 years of related experience, or 5 years of experience with a PhD, or 13 years of relevant experience in lieu of a degree
  • Experience with Linux Red Hat 7 operating system
  • A current Security+ or other equivalent IAT Level I certification
  • Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain an in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance and a minimum of an interim secret clearance is required to start

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous experience performing integration of software components and hardware items
  • Experience working in an agile work environment.
  • GMD or other missile defense experience
  • Previous experience performing performance or log analysis
  • Previous experience performing fault tree analysis
  • Previous experience working with a Missile Defense Agency (MDA) customer
  • Previous experience working with Atlassian DevOps tool suite
  • Previous experience working with MatLab, Unix scripting, or Ansible
