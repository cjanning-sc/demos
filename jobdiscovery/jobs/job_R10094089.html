ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Principal Graphic Artist to join our Melbourne, FL Creative Services team of qualified, highly engaged, and enthusiastic communications professionals focused on the promotion and publicity of our people, products and the mission they support.


Qualified candidates will be prepared to thrive in a highly active environment that demands collaboration, agility, resourcefulness, innovative thinking and use of tools to effectively communicate to employees, customers, the public, industry, stakeholders and others. This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida reporting to the Creative Services Manager. Qualified candidates will have at least 6 years of work experience in graphic design, extensive knowledge of the Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office platforms and the ability to work on Mac and PC operating systems. The candidate will serve as a strategic communications partner and must have demonstrated experience working multiple projects 

Customer satisfaction and being a strategic communications partner is a top priority. Qualified candidates will have demonstrated experience and a creative mind to work with colleagues in the business and bring their ideas to life, as a strategic communications partner.  All while working multiple projects, performing to tight deadlines, being attentive to details and solid communication, design and time management skills.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree and 6 years of experience in Graphic Design OR an additional 4 years of experience can be substituted in lieu of the degree

  • Experienced user of the Adobe Creative Suite of applications: Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator; additional Adobe applications are a plus

  • Proficient using Microsoft PowerPoint, Word and Excel

  • Experience with design and layout of print, including brochures, posters, collateral, large signage

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Active DOD Secret Clearance and Special Programs Access 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DOD Secret Clearance and Special Programs Access

  • Experience rendering, illustrating and sketching

  • Experience in photography and photo processing techniques a plus

  • Experience with design and layout of digital, including email, web, and display graphics

  • Will have knowledge of file size preparation for large format design and print

  • Adobe Premiere & Adobe After effects a plus
