R10084502
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering is seeking a Software Development Analyst (eQube). The selected candidate will administer, support, maintain, and resolve incidents on enterprise-wide systems using eQube application software. They will be involved in supervised planning of system maintenance and upgrade deployments as well as responsible for meeting software compliance standards. This position can be located in one of the following 4 locations: Redondo Beach, CA; San Diego, CA; Palmdale, CA; or Melbourne, FL.

Job responsibilities will include providing IT-focused administration and end-user support in areas of best practice development, operations support, and problem resolution for the engineering community and serving as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) in eQube.

Additional responsibilities will include:

  • Administering and implementing eQube software.

  • Providing end-user support of eQube software and its interfaces to Teamcenter.

  • Performing root cause analysis, debugging problems, and then working with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence.

  • Monitoring, addressing, and resolving eQube production environment incidents, service requests, workflows, client install packages, etc., collaboratively with the PLM development and administration teams.

  • Documenting all aspects of system support so that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting eQube.

  • Providing demos and training to users if/when required.

  • Supporting business readiness and change management activities prior to go-live and in production.

This position may be filled at Level 2 or Level 3, depending on the qualifications outlined below.

Basic Qualifications for a Level 2:

  • Associate's STEM degree with 4 years of experience, or Bachelor’s STEM degree with 2 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 6 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.

  • Knowledge of Windows operating system and server administration tools, databases, and software application

  • 0-2 years of eQube support experience.

  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret security clearance within a period of time as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Ability to obtaina Security + certification within a period of time as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

  • The ability to work effectively within teams where other team members may be located throughout the country and influencing others to action who are not direct reports.

Basic Qualifications for a Level 3:

  • Associate's STEM degree with 7 years of experience, or Bachelor’s STEM degree with 5 years of experience, or Master's STEM degree with 3 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 9 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.

  • Knowledge of Windows operating system and server administration tools, databases, and software application

  • Ability to obtain a Security + certification within a period of time as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.
