ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Systems Engineering Manager 2 based out of Ocean Springs, MS. In this role at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter.  Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation.  The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What you’ll get to do:

The Systems Engineering Manager 2 is responsible for providing functional leadership and direction to approximately 10 to 15 system performance and structural analysts.  The role is dual-hat; responsible for both functional management (20% of the time) and program duties (80% of the time).  Responsibilities include leading a diverse team in the areas of modeling & simulation, system performance, and structural analysis on machinery and/or launcher programs.  The job also includes management of time keeping, performance reviews, staffing and resource planning, training, and team engagement for their section.

Responsibilities also include:

  • Increasing systems engineering competencies through development and strategic hiring
  • Balancing employee needs with business goals
  • Provide project and technical oversight for system performance and structural analysis activities performed by the group including structural dynamics, shock & vibrations, thermal & fluid dynamics, CFD, heat transfer, linear and nonlinear FEA, and system simulation model and analytical code development
  • Maintain and update the analytical capabilities models & simulation tools
  • Provide ownership of and direction to the design processes used in the group
  • Provide proposal inputs and review proposal content for both internal and external customers
  • Basic understanding of the Design, Manufacturing and Test Integration process
  • Support department initiatives, recruiting, technology roadmap creation, etc.
