ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking an Engineer Systems as part of the T4 Systems Engineering Integration & Test team in our Chandler, AZ office.

 

Role Description & Responsibilities:

  • Serves as a Systems Engineer for the T4 Program requiring the candidate to function in a dynamic multi-disciplinary environment

  • Should possess strong technical problem solving, written and verbal communication skills to generate and document system level launch vehicle requirements, perform vehicle performance assessments and participate in system level verification/data review activities

  • Expected to regularly interface with program management, engineering staff and external customers

  • Should be willing to occasionally work during odd hours (nights/weekends) and occasionally longer than normal (>40 hrs/wk) to meet deadlines

  • Should possess a strong resolve to drive tasks to completion autonomously

  • Implements and manages system requirements in the Requirements Management databases (DOORS)

  • Provides data for and assists in risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes

  • Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, and failure/anomaly resolution

  • Supports the testing and/or evaluation/validation of one or more components of a system or subsystem analytical model or actual system implementation

  • Prepares and presents materials at program major milestone meetings

 

Basic Qualifications:

  • 2 Years with Bachelors in Science; 0 Years with Masters

  • Candidate must have a minimum of 2 years of related work experience

  • Candidate must have the ability to obtain DoD Secret level security clearance which generally requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite

  • Ability to travel is required (<10%)

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • A strong experienced-based and academic foundation in Systems Engineering fundamentals is desired

  • Experience with ACME Engineering Space Systems LVBU Systems Engineering standards/guidelines and expectations is desired

  • Experience with external customer interfacing (meetings, presentation, negotiation) is desired

  • System testing and launch operations experience is desired

  • Experience with Agile, Jira, and Scrum is desired
