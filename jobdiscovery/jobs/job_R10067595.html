ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Software Engineering Department Manager, to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Oklahoma, OK.

This Department Manager reports directly to the Eastern Region Software Engineering Director. You will be responsible for the software engineers across the entire program lifecycle.  You will be responsible for Software Development in accordance with Sector Engineering procedures, plans staffing requirements and is the primary interface to the Software Directorate to ensure staffing needs are met. You will set operating objectives and provide mentorship to first line Section Managers based on Engineering goals, objectives, and company policy.

A successful candidate will have the qualities and essential functions:

  • Excellent communication, mentoring, interpersonal skills, and the ability to collaborate with senior management, peers, and employees
  • Provides ownership for the people, processes, and tools that support divisions and programs achieve their missions
  • Accountability for recruiting, staffing and retaining talent to deliver on the objectives
  • Supporting proposal work, independent technical reviews, program standup and audits, and special assignments on an as needed basis
  • Owning and leading improvements across employee engagement, hiring, development, training, attraction, and retention of top talent
  • Mentor, develop, and train your section managers, and overall employee base.
  • Coordinates modification records for management control
  • Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor
  • Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities
  • Interface with all levels of leadership to maintain and exceed health and performance for all programs
  • Excellent communication (written, oral, and presentation) and engagement skills

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM related field with 9 years’ Computer Engineering or Software Engineering experience, or master’s with 7 Computer Engineering or Software Engineering years, or PhD with 4 years Computer Engineering or Software Engineering experience.
  • Experience as a Software Project Manager or Integrated Product Team Lead
  • Experience in all phases of the Software Development lifecycles
  • Experience in planning staffing requirements
  • At least 5 years of management experience such as Section Manager, Staffing Manager, Assistant DM
  • Knowledge and familiar with all phases of the software development life cycle
  • In depth understanding of NGAS Engineering processes
  • Must have an active DoD Secret (or higher) clearance (with a background investigation within the past 6 years or enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top-Secret Clearance and Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in STEM related field and/or MBA
  • Understand NGAS Sector Engineering procedures
  • Experience as a Software Project Manager for teams greater than 50
  • Experience as a certified Agile Software Development Coach
  • Experience in Leadership and management of a large team.
  • Familiarity with ACME Engineering Software Development processes, CMMI Level 3 or higher, AS9100, or DO-178
  • Experience in collaboration with other leads (SEIT, Avionics, Vehicle and Test) in a team-oriented environment to achieve program goals
  • Green or Black Belt Lean / Six Sigma training and experiences
  • Relevant experience from the Defense and/or Aeronautics industry
  • Active TS Clearance or Program Access
