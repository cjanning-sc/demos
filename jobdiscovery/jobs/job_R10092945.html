ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Engineer Network/Principal Engineer Network to join the System Test Team (STT).   This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   Final grade level offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The qualified applicant will become part of ACME Engineering’s team supporting the Air Force’s Ground Based Strategic Deterrent program. Duties of this position include development, production and deployment of network equipment hardware, software, and systems for the system test team. The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers, and other engineers.

Supports designs and plans for network communications systems. Drafts specifications and detailed schematics for network architecture. Drafts specific detailed information for hardware and software selection, implementation techniques and tools for the most efficient solution to meet business needs, including present and future capacity requirements. Supports testing of network design. Develops technical expertise in all areas of network and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc. Supports evaluation of new communications technologies to enhance capabilities of the network.
