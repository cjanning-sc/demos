Sr Principal Engineer Thermal (AHT)

1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering is seeking a Sr. Principal Thermal Engineer located in Northridge, CA.

In this role, the selected candidate will be responsible for conducting thermal analyses on various components of a missile system.  Candidates must be able to perform thermal analyses with less supervision from Experienced thermal analysts or lead, also able to mentoring new / non-experienced engineers /w SINDA software skill plus, good communication skills.

Tasks include:

  • Reading customer SOW/Technical Specifications and deriving boundary conditions for thermal models
  • Creating system level thermal models of assemblies and detailed thermal models of subassemblies and individual parts
  • Creating thermal models of circuit card assemblies down to the component junction level
  • Performing steady state and transient thermal analyses to predict temperatures, heat flows, and heat sinking requirements
  • Working with multi-disciplinary teams of engineers to achieve successful design and fabrication programs/projects
  • Prepare for PDR/CDR
  • work schedule 4 X 10
  • Must be on site

Other roles/responsibilities will include (but are not limited to):

  • Perform hand calculation to verify thermal models
  • Design thermal environmental tests to verify thermal models
  • Preparing and presenting results/findings to peers and customers
  • Interface with suppliers and customers on an as needed basis

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering
  • 9+ years of experience in thermodynamics, heat transfer and aero heating modeling techniques
  • Must be proficient in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook)
  • Proficient in FEMAP / SINDA (or other commercial thermal analysis codes)
  • Must be able to obtain a secret security clearance within a timeframe set forth by management; U.S. Citizenship required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • SINDA skill plus
  • Knowledge of MINIVER or other aero heating codes a plus
  • Current Active Secret Clearance
