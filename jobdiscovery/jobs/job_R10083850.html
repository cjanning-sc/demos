ACME Engineering, Mission Systems is looking for an experienced Program Manager to support the tremendous amount of growth within the Electronic Warfare Systems (EWS) business unit.

The Program Manager: Electronic Warfare Product Line will report directly to the EW Growth and Capture Manager and will be responsible for supporting multiple EW Product Line activities.

This position requires the ability to manage multiple priorities while maintaining and fostering effective communication. The candidate must be able to lead a cross-functional organization, maintaining continuous alignment with internal stakeholders and critical product line suppliers. The Product Line Program Manager is responsible for coordination and management of all EWS funded Capital projects, from inception through completion while leading multiple EW Product Line initiatives. Finally, the preferred candidate will be a self-motivated, quick learner with a demonstrated track record of increasing responsibility and successful project execution.

Primary responsibilities of the Program Manager: Electronic Warfare Product Line will include but are not limited to:

· Oversee execution of EWS capital projects

· Oversee implementation of DMS resolution across the product line

· Lead EWS second sourcing initiative for future product line risk reduction

· Spearhead lead-time reduction initiatives across supply base

· Lead producibility and affordability initiatives across the product line

· Collect and provide monthly updated product sales forecasts to internal and external stakeholders in support of proactive investment in capacity planning

· Planning / Budgeting for cross-product / cross-functional rental equipment needs

· Information System Owner for Classified IR&D Network

· Coordinate EWS OU cross-program dependencies

· Coordinate EWS OU and EWS NCTA Work Load Forecasting demand inputs

This position is located within the exciting Greater Chicago Area and can be considered for a hybrid work arrangement, requiring a fixed percentage of time on site.

Preferred Qualifications:

· Current Secret clearance with the ability to obtain additional clearances, such as Special Access Requirements

· Sourcing/Supply Chain Management experience that includes strategic planning

· A technical background or demonstration of ability to execute complex projects

· Earned Value Management (EVMS) experience

· Project Management Certification (PMP)

Basic Qualifications:

· Prior Project or Program Management experience, ideally supporting DoD contracts

· Ability to manage multiple projects within cost, schedule, and technical requirements

· Excellent interpersonal skills with demonstrated success aligning cross-functional teams and building effective relationships with internal and external stakeholders or customers.

· Strong business acumen and strategic thinking skills.

· The ability to establish and apply a technical performance baseline to monitor and assess program status against key performance indicators

· The ability to quickly identify and resolve issues with strategic solutions or process improvements that support long-term objectives

· Bachelor's Degree and 5 years’, or Master’s and 3 years’ of relevant experience will be required for consideration
