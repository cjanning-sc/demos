ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector, Networked Information Solutions (NIS) Division, Growth and Mission Solutions is seeking a Manager 1/2, Mission Analysis. In this exciting and challenging role, the selected individual will lead a team of mission analysts that shapes both NIS internal investments and customer acquisition decision, with the objective of accelerating overall division growth. The selected candidate must be able to manage a team effectively and engage with a broad range of internal and external stakeholders.

Acceptable locations for this role are: Northern Virginia, Baltimore, MD, and San Diego, CA

Job Description

Roles and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Lead a high-performing team of mission analysts including managing workflow, backlog, load balancing, and support for internal/external engagaments
  • Perform mission analysis directly as an individual contributor
  • Ensure that appropriate facilities, tools, and processes are in place to support execution of the defined program of analysis
  • Support the Director, Mission Architecture and Vice President, Growth and Mission Solutions in implementing the required process and culture to enable analytical excellence
  • Work with analytical organizations across the sector, company, and key stakeholders to deliver impactful results
  • Train and mentor new analysts
  • Communicate process and results effectively to key internal and external stakeholders

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications for Level 1:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 5+ years of work experience or Master’s Degree and 3+ years
  • Direct experience with analytical tools such as CAMEO, AFSIM, STK, and MATLAB
  • Track record of strong analytical performance including effective communication of results
  • Project management experience
  • U.S. Citizen with active TS clearance

Basic Qualifications for Level 2:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years of work experience or Master’s Degree and 7+ years
  • Direct experience with analytical tools such as CAMEO, AFSIM, STK, and MATLAB
  • Track record of strong analytical performance including effective communication of results
  • Project management experience
  • U.S. Citizen with active TS clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with agile methodologies
  • Experience with digital collaboration tools including Confluence and Jira
