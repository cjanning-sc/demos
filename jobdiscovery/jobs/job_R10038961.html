ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Manufacturing Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals, and be a part of our Payload and Ground Systems division in our Commerce, CA facility. Commerce is the leading supplier of pressure vessels and tank hardware to the aerospace industry. The Manufacturing Engineer will work on assigned programs on non-recurring work as well as provide technical support on the shop floor.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and improve manufacturing processes to meet challenging hardware requirements

  • Review engineering designs and drawings for accuracy and manufacturability

  • Manage Manufacturing Bill of Materials through the use of MRP

  • Work alongside the manufacturing teams to ensure that overall quality, delivery, and cost objectives are achieved

  • Communicate manufacturing plans and processes to internal and external customers through Manufacturing Readiness Reviews

  • Provide technical guidance and liaison to operations, assist supervisors on new techniques, and  determination of causes of rejected parts

  • Create manufacturing plans and detailed work instructions

  • Provide redlines and/or updating of Manufacturing Work Instructions on the shop floor

  • Specify all necessary special processes, equipment, and fixtures required for manufacturing on assigned programs

  • Continuously work to improve manufacturing methods, efficiency and product quality

  • Participate in continuous improvement projects

  • Assist in tool design when required to help support operations by developing tooling concepts and using of CAD software to model and release of tool drawings

  • Monitor quality discrepancies, identify trends, investigate problems, and implement corrective actions

  • Evaluate manufacturing processes by applying knowledge of product design, fabrication, assembly, tooling, and materials; conferring with equipment vendors; soliciting observations from operators

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM field and 3 years of related experience

  • Must be able to define problems, collect data, establish facts, draw valid conclusions, and demonstrate the ability to determine critical parameters and develop process controls

  • Proven multi-tasker and self-starter with excellent verbal and written communication skills, critical thinking, logical reasoning and problem solving skills that predict potential technical risks (internal and suppliers)

  • Experience applying and implementing lean methodologies in manufacturing/production environments including root-cause and Ishikawa analysis

  • Practical application of AS9100, ISO 9001-2008, AS9145  or similar quality systems

  • Proficiency with MS Office required; MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint

  • Proficiency with Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software required

  • Proficient in reading engineering drawings and interpretation of GD&T.

  • Working knowledge of machining processes; lathe, milling, CNC programming

  • Working knowledge of welding and heat treating processes

  • US citizenship required


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Siemens NX

  • Proficiency with MS Access

  • Experience in any of the following:

    • Aerospace manufacturing environment (3+ years)

    • Heat treating of titanium alloys (3+ years)

    • Machining of titanium alloys (3+ years)

    • Welding of Titanium  (3+ years)

  • Working knowledge of composite materials and processes

  • Understanding of 5S and Lean Manufacturing experience
