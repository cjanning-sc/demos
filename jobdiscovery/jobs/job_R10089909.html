The Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD), within the ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector, is seeking a Finance Manager 2 to join our diverse team. The position is located in Redondo Beach, California and reports to the senior SSSD Finance Manager.  It requires a solid understanding and working knowledge of financial planning and reporting and involves day-to-day supervision, guidance, and development to exempt financial analysts within the SSSD Controller’s Office organization.

This position requires the incumbent to possess a Department of Defense (DoD) active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI).  

Responsibilities will include:

  • Financial planning (Annual Operating Plan/Long Range Strategic Plan and monthly forecasts)
  • Financial reporting & financial statement analysis (Income Statement/Balance Sheet/Cash Flow Statement)
  • Estimate at Complete (EAC) profitability review and analysis
  • Sarbanes-Oxley Control program
  • Treasury/cash management

Basic Qualifications:

  • Active DoD TS Clearance or SCI Access
  • Bachelor’s Degree with 6 years of experience in any combination of business management, finance and or accounting OR a Master's Degree and 4 years of stated experience
  • Working knowledge of financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement)
  • High level of proficiency with MS Office tools, especially Excel
  • Leadership experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree
  • Current or previous experience in Aerospace & Defense financial planning and reporting
  • SAP ERP experience
  • IBM Cognos Analytics experience
