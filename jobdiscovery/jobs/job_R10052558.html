ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a System Engineer to support an Operations and Maintenance facility located in Alice Springs, Australia.  The successful candidate will provide technical planning, cost-benefit analysis, and integration support for operational systems.  This System Engineer will also serve as an interface to Site and CONUS engineering organizations.  The principal duties of the position are:

  • Provide engineering support to include; requirements definition/evaluation, planning, coordination, identifying and addressing impacts, generating documentation, and test support.
  • Research, prepare and present baseline change proposals to internal and external customers
  • Plan, design and support execution of installation and integration activities addressing operational equipment obsolescence and delivery of new systems/capabilities
  • Develop and coordinate technical and conceptual trade studies between Site and Factory Operations and Maintenance teams
  • Support system engineering functions for the Program including management of project schedules, program risks, opportunities, issues and baseline changes
  • Assess program operations and engineering activities to ensure maximum availability, maintainability and overall performance

A comprehensive overseas compensation package is offered with this position—relocation assistance will be available.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors degree in a STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) discipline and 9 years of related experience OR 7 years with a Masters in STEM OR 4 years experience with a Ph.D in STEM
  • The successful candidate should have excellent written and oral communication skills and strong organization skills
  • Strong interpersonal team building skills are also required
  • Must be able to thrive and be an effective contributor in a dynamic operational environment working across many organizations and interfaces

  • Active and in scope TS/SCI with CI Polygraph

Preferred Qualifications:

  • AMP/ATP/Mission 1 experience is highly desired
  • Experience with System Engineering foundations and concepts
  • Familiarity with Site Integration Standards

A two year overseas tour commitment is required.  Candidate must pass security/polygraph screening.
