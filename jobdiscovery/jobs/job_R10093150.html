1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering’s Cable Manufacturing Engineering team is seeking a Manufacturing Engineer to join the team in Launch Vehicles at our Chandler, AZ facility. 

Job Responsibilities

Emphasis will be working closely with new programs on the cable mock up/fab tools. Prefer some cable experience to communicate with Program Res and cable techs. Requires strong critical thinking skills, strong organization and communication skills and the ability to read and understand cable drawings. Will be trained to work IPCRs, MES (Manufacturing Engineering System) plan alterations, up rev plans, and create design change instructions in MES. Will work build issues with the cable production floor. Must have good organizational and communication skills.  Will be asked to present processes to small groups.

Specific duties may include:

  • Design manufacturing processes, procedures and production layouts for assemblies, equipment installation, processing, machining and material handling.

  • Design arrangement of machines within plant facilities to ensure most efficient and productive layout.

  • Design sequence of operations and specify procedures for the fabrication of tools and equipment and other functions that affect product performance.

  • Adapt machine or equipment design to factory and production conditions.

  • May incorporate inspection and test requirements into the production plan.

  • Inspect performance of machinery, equipment, and tools to verify their efficiency, and investigate and initiate corrective action of problems and deficiencies to ensure product quality.

  • Develop manufacturing processes that are applicable to statistical process control, and may develop those techniques.

  • Provide guidance to engineering regarding design concepts and specification requirements to best utilize equipment and manufacturing techniques.

  • Ensure processes and procedures are in compliance with regulations.

Required Qualifications:

  • Minimum Education / Experience: Bachelors of Science in Engineering +2 years of experience; Masters Degree + 0 years of experience

  • US Citizenship

  • Ability to read and understand cable drawings

  • Ability to travel up to 10%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Cable experience

  • Familiarity with Manufacturing Engineering System (MES)

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

  • Experience presenting to groups

  • Ability to obtain Security Clearance
