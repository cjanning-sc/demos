ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an immediate opportunity for a Senior Principal Project Management position on the F-35 Program based in Redondo Beach, CA.  In this position, your key roles and responsibilities will include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Provide executive support to the F-35 Program Office creating briefs, white papers, and other deliverables for decision making and status reviews by higher management at the Air Dominance Division, Aeronautic, and NG Corporate offices and by customers.
  • Act as the liaison between the F-35 Program and counterparts on the Air Dominance Division staff, the Aeronautics Sector staff, and Functional Homerooms, particularly as the Program spokesperson for the Program Office.
  • Assume the role as the Lead Program Integrator to lead the integration effort on the F-35 Program and provide leadership to the various Integrators throughout the Program to ensure the unimpeded flow of information and an integrated, coordinated work environment between the Program Office, Integrated Product Teams (IPTs), and Functions on the Program.
  • Gather, analyze, interpret, and present Program data to assess and report on the status of the F-35 Program to upper management and to develop and present solutions to Program-wide issues.
  • Develop, disseminate, and implement Program goals / objectives and ensure that various Integrated Product Teams (IPTs) and Functions on the Program are consistently striving toward them.
  • Initiate and complete projects as required to further Program objectives and manage resources.

Successful candidates will have the following key attributes:

  • Proactive – anticipates requirements from higher management and initiate and complete actions required to meet those requirements.
  • Perceptive – sees the “big picture” but is also able to focus on the details and follows all of them through to completion.
  • Composed – maintains a “level head” in the extremely dynamic, fast-paced, and demanding environment on the F-35 Program, especially when working closely with executive management on projects with highly compressed timelines and tight deadlines.
  • Reliable – becomes a key resource and the “go-to” person for the executive leadership in the F-35 Program Office.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors Degree and a minimum of 10 years work experience or 8 years with Masters of Business Administration (MBA) degree
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications (PowerPoint, Word, Excel)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Analyst, Manufacturing and Program development experience
  • Masters Preferred
