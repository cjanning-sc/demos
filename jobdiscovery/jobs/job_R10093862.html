Sr. Principal Property/Asset Management Analyst

PGSD has an opening for a Property/Asset Management Analyst to perform multiple functions including contract property closeout, physical inventory, records and reports for certain NG Space programs. Listed below are the various functions and tasks for this position:

 Candidate will be responsible for overseeing management systems, policies and procedures to ensure contractual and continual accountability of government, other customer and company assets under the responsibility of various NG Space suppliers/ subcontractors.

  • Conduct property surveillance reviews at supplier/ subcontractor sites.
  • Provide various material and tagged asset reports.
  • Preparation of contract transfer documentation
  • Preparation and submittal of property financial reports
  • Coordinating physical inventory process
  • Property record review and inventory updates in property database
  • High level of interaction with both internal and external customers
  • Assist in Contractor Self-Assessment (CSA) reviews.
  • Collaborate with Buyers, Subcontract Administrators, Property Focal Points (PFPs) to ensure accountability of assets is maintained.
  • Assist suppliers/ subcontractors in the property management life cycle recovery plans, as needed.
  • Assist other team members, as necessary.

 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 9 years of related Property/Asset Management experience; 7 years of experience with a master's degree; 13 years of experience in lieu of a degree
  • Ability to interpret and formulate independent judgment based on federal regulations, FAR 52-245.1 and applicable DFARS
  • Experience with industry leading best practices, techniques, and standards as they pertain to property and asset management.
  • U.S. Citizen with TS/SCI Clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

National Property Management Association Certifications (CPPs, CPPA, CPPM)
