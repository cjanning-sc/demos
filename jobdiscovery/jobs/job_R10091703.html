ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Derive, Allocate, Update and Maintain requirements and specifications
  • Develop Weapon System Architecture Model and Requirements Verification Architecture Model.
  • Develop and manage interfaces.
  • Perform requirements analysis, develop source data for requirements
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Requirements Management that includes Model Base Systems Engineering (MBSE), Requirement Decomposition, Functional Block Diagrams and Specification Trees
  • Systems Design, Systems Integration, Analysis and Trade Studies

  • Medical, Dental & Vision coverage

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal level or level Sr. Principal level.

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline
  • Systems Engineer requires a minimum 2 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 0 years with a Master’s
  • Principal Systems Engineer requires a minimum of 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master in Science or 0 years with a PHD
  • U.S. Citizenship and an Active Secret Security Clearance or higher awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years
  • Understanding of systems engineering principles and processes
  • Experience working in an Agile team
  • Proficiency in MS Office
  • Principal System Engineer requires working experience in Model Base Systems Engineering (MBSE) e.g. Cameo model development
    • Experience in MBSE techniques, including requirements management; interface control and verification; logical, physical and functional architecture; trade studies; and analysis of alternatives.
    • Understanding of SysML, UML, or other descriptive constructs/tools.
    • Understanding of the following specialty areas: nuclear surety, cybersecurity, flight safety
    • Mature requirements definition experience including definition of verification plans

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Experience using tools to model large systems with multiple developers.
  • Experience in the military aerospace development environment is preferred.
  • Experience with weapon systems
  • Experience with Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) including model development
  • Experience with Agile Development
  • Experience working in an Agile team or even leading a Scrum team
