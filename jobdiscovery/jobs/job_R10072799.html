ACME Engineering Space Components is seeking a Quality Inspection Manager to join our team.  The Process Center Leader is responsible for compliance through delivery to internal customers and secondarily to external customers. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

Overview

Our team is responsible for various quality inspection responsibilities including receiving supplied material/parts, in-process inspection of product, and final acceptance product.  The Quality Inspection Manager will also be responsible for reviewing quality inspection processes, work with operations on schedule completions, assist with customer audits, and be the Quality Lead on monitoring Suppliers. 

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Support all operation inspection needs and communicate quality issues as appropriate.

  • Assure technicians are working tasks that align with the schedule.

  • Works with Quality Inspectors on career development and training.

  • Resolves employee and/or resource priorities across Operations and Production Control

  • Coordinate within the Quality team or functions as needed to address obstacles preventing technicians from completing their work

  • Responsible for monitoring Suppliers, reviewing Purchase requisitions, and assuring purchased materials meet requirements by developing the inspection plans. 

  • Responsible for implementation of proactive quality initiatives or projects to support program affordability and technical compliance

  • Additional responsibilities may include calibration of tooling, gaging, and other equipment on-site or by coordination with outside suppliers with equipment maintenance.

  • Accountable to meet or exceed the Quality Assurance, and support the Operational, safety, quality, delivery, employee and cost (SQDEC) expectations of external and internal customers across all AOX programs supported by (either Quality Assurance or Quality Engineering).

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma or GED with 6+ years applicable experience or a Bachelor’s degree and 2+ years’ experience (such as schedules, labs, quality, manufacturing)

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a top secret clearance

  • US Citizenship required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in an Aerospace or Composite Materials manufacturing environment

  • Experience with Quality Management Systems e.g., AS9100
