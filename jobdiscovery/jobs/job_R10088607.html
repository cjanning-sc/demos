This position requires an Agile Product Owner to support the Threat Products and Services (TPS) product line of the DE Task Order on the Specialized Warfighter Development Contract (SWDC) program. The job location is at Schriever SFB, Colorado Springs, CO. 

Candidate will support a team of systems engineers, test engineers, and software developers to plan, design, develop, test, and document feature capabilities.  Primary responsibilities include developing requirements, prioritizing the team backlog, and documenting capabilities. Additionally, this position will require coordination across operational, simulation, and user communities to design and deploy new features and enhancements to new and existing applications.  Secondary responsibilities will include interfacing with TPS customers and presenting technical briefings to TPS stakeholders.
