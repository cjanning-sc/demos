ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program.

What you’ll get to do:

Reviews field support requirements and recommends tool and test equipment. Establishes calibration requirements for field systems/equipment including all maintenance tasks. Performs maintenance and maintainability demonstrations for customers. Reviews handbooks for technical adequacy. Assists in the development of maintenance engineering and logistics support. Plans and contributes to proposal efforts.

Additional Responsibilities include:

  • Assessment, analysis and risk for identified mission-critical functions and critical components
  • Support courses of action based on knowledge and experience, initiative, guidance, and established regulations and policies
  • Contributes to program plans, goals, objectives and milestones
  • Organize and conducts studies for various operational initiatives
  • Participate in a variety of working groups and customer meetings; ensure communication of risk environment with stakeholders
  • Execute completion Statement of Work requirements, Program Milestone Exit Criteria, and program maturity commitments
  • Self-starters compelled to take action in the workplace without requiring prompting from supervisors
  • Support LST with other duties as assigned
