The team in Oxnard, CA has an opening for a Quality Inspector 3 / 4 to join the PGS Division. The Quality Inspector 3 / 4 will perform required inspections (visual, dimensional, load tests, etc.) on mechanical, electrical or structural components or systems to ensure conformance with the quality requirements for spacecraft and associated systems, in support of construction, overhaul, integration and repair contracts. This position reports to a Supervisor or Manager, and works under general supervision and/or the direction of a Lead Inspector.

Responsibilities:

  • Uses predetermined methods, operations, setups and prescribed specifications to inspect visually in-process and completed products such as electronic units and subsystems, precision electromechanical assemblies or mechanical units, subassemblies, structural flaws, internal defects, and missing welds
  • Perform required inspections (visual, dimensional, load tests, etc.) on mechanical, electrical or structural components or systems to ensure conformance with the quality requirements for spacecraft and associated systems, in support of construction, overhaul, integration and repair contracts.
  • Uses various measuring devices, such as caliper, micrometer, comparator, gages, pin gages, and other inspection tools including Coordinate Measuring Machine
  • This job has decision-making responsibilities with regard to the acceptance or rejection of work and tests performed and the determination of measuring equipment used.
  • Inspects and performs tests on mechanical, electrical or structural components and assemblies related to spacecraft and sub-assemblies
  • Inspections vary from close tolerance dimensional checks to general surveillance of the work in progress.
  • Accepts, rejects, or reworks defective or malfunctioning units or systems
  • Works from blueprints, diagrams, dial indicators, preset micrometers, scales, fixtures, customer specifications, drawing or inspection instructions and checklists
  • Monitors and verifies quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures
  • Performs line clearances after each lot to ensure all materials from the previous lot have been removed
  • Performs other duties such as calibration maintenance and/or records maintenance as directed
