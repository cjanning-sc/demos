ACME Engineering is seeking a Manufacturing Engineer to join our Launch Vehicles team located in Chandler, AZ.

This position will be responsible for implementing, supporting, and sustaining the use of Manufacturing Engineering System (MES) work instructions for new production programs. The role will require communication between all stakeholders within the design & production lifecycle to ensure manufacturing processes are accounted for early on. A deep understanding of Integration & Test (I&T) procedures, such as: build sequence, tooling design, work cell layout(s), material handling, troubleshooting/corrective action, safety protocols, and quality assurance is required. This role will also assist in conducting Manufacturing Readiness Assessments & Manufacturing Readiness Level (MRA/MRL) evaluations. Frequent assessments of internal & external suppliers will be performed in accordance to Launch Vehicle & DoD standards. Knowledge of Model-Based Definition (MBD) concepts, Computer-Aided Design (CAD) tools, Product Manufacturing Information (PMI), and Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T) is highly desired.

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelors of Science in Engineering +2 years of experience; Masters Degree + 0 years of experience

  • Experience with Manufacturing Engineering System (MES)

  • Ability to obtain clearance

  • US Citizenship

  • Ability to travel up to 25%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Understanding of Integration & Test (I&T)

  • Familiarity with Manufacturing Readiness Assessments & Manufacturing Readiness Level (MRA/MRL)

  • Knowledge of Model-Based Definition (MBD) concepts, Computer-Aided Design (CAD) tools, Product Manufacturing Information (PMI), and Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T)
