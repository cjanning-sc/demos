ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Software Development Environment (SDE) Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Huntsville, AL.

Position Overview:

  

The selected Principal SDE Administrator will provide support for the Command and Control, Battle Management, and Communications (C2BMC) program. The selected candidate will support the SDE Team to ensure the C2BMC software development and integration staff have the required tools and processes in place to develop and integrate the C2BMC system. 

Essential Functions:

  • Implement and maintain an integrated software development environment
  • Perform necessary system administration functions to support the SDE team and SDE applications
  • Research new technology and tools to determine their applicability to the SDE and support trade studies by providing data to determine which technologies provide the most cost effective, lowest implementation risk while providing a high performance SDE
  • Integrate new tools into the SDE, documenting the configuration and use to assist with training for the developers using the toolset
  • Perform upgrades and maintenance in the toolset
  • Work with Configuration Management and Program Operations to manage software licenses for the development sites

.

The selected candidate must possess the ability to work in a fast paced environment providing coordination of activities within the Development organization. The candidate must be a self starter who is able to initiate research and evaluate new software development tools, processes and procedures.

BQs for a Principal SDE System Administrator:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 5 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree and 3 years of related experience, or 0 years of experience with a PhD.
  • Previous experience with evaluating different toolsets and providing recommendations for use
  • Experience with Windows and Linux system administration
  • Familiarity with Software Configuration Management tools and procedures
  • Experience with Software Development using Java
  • CompTIA Security+ (or equivalent) DoD 8570.01M IAT Level II – Certification
  • A current active U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Ansible Automation Platform, including Ansible Tower
  • Experience with VM Horizon View
  • Hands on experience with some software development tools such as Git, Ant, Ansible, Maven, Nexus, JUnit, Confluence, SonarQube, JIRA, Jenkins and Fortify Ability to read and troubleshoot application source code
  • A current active U.S. Government DoD Top Secret security clearance.
