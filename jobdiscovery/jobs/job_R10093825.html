ACME Engineering has a need for PC Network Support Technicians in our Aurora, CO location.

Roles and Responsibilities will include but aren't limited to:

  • Resolves end user IT issues and provide end user IT services surrounding computer hardware, software, network, and telecommunications systems while demonstrating expertise in customer service and technical knowledge.

  • Perform lower-level system updates such as account creations, phone configuration, network device registration, etc.

  • Records, maintains, and updates records in the Incident Management system. Performs analysis of problems and assist with corrective action to restore functionality, and works with technical, customer service or development staff to resolve recurring problems and issues with applications and/or products.

  • Documents and submits problem resolutions to the knowledge database to assist other Help Desk personnel with problem resolutions.

This position can be filled at either a level 2 or level 3.

Basic Qualifications - Level 2:

  • Bachelors degree with 0 years experience; or High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience.

  • Candidates must have a current Top Secret security clearance with SCI access completed within the last 5 years to be considered

  • ability to obtain a Polygraph

  • Current DOD 8570 IAT Level II certification (ex: Security + CE) certification, or obtain within 6 months of hire

Basic Qualifications - Level 3:

  • Bachelors degree with 2 years experience; or High School diploma or equivalent and 6 years additional education and/or related experience

  • Candidates must have a current Top Secret security clearance with SCI access completed within the last 5 years to be considered

  • Ability to obtain a Polygraph

  • Current DOD 8570 IAT Level II certification (ex: Security + CE) certification, or obtain within 6 months of hire

Preferred Qualifications - Levels 2 and 3:

  • Current TS/SCI with poly

  • Current DOD 8570 IAT Level II certification

  • Certified Help Desk Professional, MCP, MCSE, A+, Linux certified or certificates in related disciplines

  • Strong troubleshooting abilities are a must.

  • Must be customer and detail oriented and possess good decision-making ability.

  • General understanding of Computing and Storage Systems and Networking

  • Experienced in providing remote support to end users utilizing remote desktop tools and walking users through troubleshooting steps over the phone.

  • Must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills.

  • Knowledge and understanding of customer service techniques. Prefer knowledge of Microsoft Office automation solutions, and a willingness to learn new tools and technologies and take on new responsibilities as assigned.
