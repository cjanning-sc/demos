ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal / Senior Principal Software Engineer to join the System Test Team (STT). This position is located in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level for position based on candidate experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will support element level testing which includes:

• Design, develop, and implement testing methods and equipment.

• Perform development of Test Automation for Software and hardware qualification

• Utilize ability to navigate requirements in DOORS

• Utilize Model Based System Engineering including DODAF and SysML principles

• Utilize technical experience in network, computer hardware and software, interconnection and/or interfacing technologies

• Collaborate with systems engineers, software engineers and lab users to ensure test requirements are met

• Develop and release engineering documentation including:

o Perform data analysis against requirements and interfaces for each software build or hardware component

o Interface with software developers and systems engineers in support of verification

o Product integration, regression, and verification testing including checkout in system test labs.

• Utilize knowledge of AGILE SW Development and test automation principles

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Job Qualifications:

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

•Principal engineer: Bachelor and 5 years of experience; 3 years with Master’s Degree; 0 years with PhD or 4 additional years in lieu of a degree. 

•Sr. Principal engineer: Bachelor and 9 years of experience; 7 years with Master’s Degree; 4 years with PhD or 4 additional years in lieu of a degree. 

•Software test automation experience

•Current DoD Secret clearance granted or re-investigated within last 6 years and ability to obtain Special Access Program.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

•Experience with System Safety Testing for Software and Hardware

•Security+ Certification

•Proficiency with Tools such as: Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, etc.

•Automated Test experience (development and execution)

•Agile experience

•Model Based System Engineering with an understanding of DODAF and SysML

•Proficiency in DOORS and Cameo

•A current DoD Top Secret/SCI level security clearance, to include a Single Scope Background Investigation, a periodic reinvestigation, or an original adjudication date completed within the last 6 years
