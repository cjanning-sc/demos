ACME Engineering Defense Systems is seeking a Principal Financial Analyst to support the division financial planning/reporting manager in the Combat Systems and Mission Readiness Division.  This position reports to the Division Financial Planning Manager and serves a key analyst role for the division.  The position is located in McLean, Virginia (May consider partial telework). 

Roles and responsibilities of the Principal Financial Analyst include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Financial planning, reporting, and analysis
  • Annual Operating Plan (AOP) duties
  • Ensure Revenue Recognition (C-901) population completeness as well as perform division reviews
  • Element of Cost and Headcount forecasting
  • Review of intercompany and subk suspense for monthly reporting
  • SOX activities
  • Mentoring and training interns and college hires
  • Other ad hoc assignments as required
  • Strong excel skills

The candidate should be well organized, schedule-driven, and have the ability to multi-task while meeting tight deadlines.  This position also requires excellent communication skills, as frequent interaction with senior Division, Sector, and BU/OU finance leadership teams as well as other support staff functions is necessary.  We are looking for a highly motivated team player to join our dynamic, fast-paced financial planning and reporting organization.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a high school diploma or GED with a minimum of 10 years of direct experience preparing financial data and performing analysis.
  • Will also consider:  
    • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Business Administration, Accounting, or a related field with 6 years of professional experience
    • Master’s degree in Finance, Business Administration, Accounting, or a related field with 4 years of related professional experience
  • Proficiency with MS Office Suite, specifically strong Excel, Word, and PowerPoint skills
  • Must be able to work onsite in McLean, VA (May consider partial telework).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience preparing and presenting complex financial analysis to varying levels of management
  • Experience with Hyperion Planning, HFM, Access databases, and intermediate/advanced Excel skills
  • Experience with variance reporting using the following reporting tools (or similar):
    • SAP
    • Business Warehouse (BW)
    • Precision/Cognos
