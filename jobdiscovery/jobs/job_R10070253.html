ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Systems Engineer / Senior​ Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

The qualified candidate will report to the Aerospace Vehicle Equipment Power and Distribution Lead and is responsible for building system engineering artifacts needed for Power and Distribution to complete First Flight Design Review, Critical Design Review, Functional Configuration Audit, and System Qualification/Verification Review.  The role entails coordination with higher level system engineers and design engineering to produce the needed documentation and set baseline system documentation in Cameo and DOORs and push artifacts through engineering approval and configuration management.

  • 401k

  • Paid Time Off
  • Health & Wellness Resources
  • Employee Discounts 

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal level or level Sr. Principal level.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics)
  • A minimum 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with PhD. (Sr. Principal level requires 9 years with a Bachelor’s degree; 7 years with a Master’s degree; or 4 years with a PhD)
  • Active DoD Secret clearance or higher
  • US Citizen eligible for Special Access Program Clearance (DoD clearance awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years)
  • Knowledge/Use of requirement management tool (DOORS or similar tool)

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Proficient in DOORs
  • Familiarity with Pspice
  • Knowledge/Use of Cameo
  • In scope DoD Secret security clearance
  • Strong leadership qualities, as well as strong verbal and written communication skills
