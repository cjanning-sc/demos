Armament Systems, a Business Unit of ACME Engineering - Defense Systems Sector, is seeking an Engineering Manager 3, to join our team in Plymouth, MN.

Responsibilities of this role:

Lead the Product Development Complex (PDC) team of managers, engineers and technicians within the Production Engineering department.  This Engineer Manager role reports directly to the Director of Production Engineering.

Specific responsibilities and desired experience include:

  • Effectively lead a diverse team of managers, engineers, and technicians in a dynamic product development environment.

  • Drive Team technical excellence within the PDC. 

  • Balance PDC group workload and staffing requirements to meet current and future program needs. 

  • Coach direct reports in the areas of career development, performance, and teamwork.   Provide training and development opportunities.  Provide continual feedback and hold mid-year and end-of-year performance communications.

  • Have experience with fast paced agile team engagement on multiple programs.

  • Maintain a “learning organization” where each team member is expected to teach and learn.

  • Plan, execute and manage department budget

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Position requires a minimum of 13 years of experience.  Would consider a Bachelor of Science in Engineering with 9 years of experience, MS with 7 years of experience or a PHD with 4 years of experience.

  • 6 years of Engineering experience in both product development and production environments.

  • Previous Team management and/or Integrated Product Team (IPT) leadership experience.

  • Must be a US Citizen and able to acquire a DoD Secret Security Clearance

  • Excellent communication skills

  • Production/Development of defense products including technical, manufacturing, cost, and schedule aspects.

  • Experience working with customer engineering (U S Government), vendors and subcontractors.

  • Proven track record of working with individuals, as well as teams, at various levels and disciplines.

  • Department team leadership, supervisory, and/or people management experience.
