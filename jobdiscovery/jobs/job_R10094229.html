Principal Cyber Systems Engineer - ESS (23-105)
R10094229

ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Cyber Systems Engineer - ESS to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Colorado Springs, CO.

Position Overview:

  • Position will involve all phases of the System Development Lifecycle for this Cyber technology.
  • This position will be responsible for the design, architecture, development, integration, unit test, installation, configuration, integration, deployment and maintenance to include: HBSS components, ePO and Win/RHEL Point Products.
  • Maintain the latest HBSS configuration, host agents and policies to enforce host-based security on system servers/work stations.
  • Perform regular required reporting, monitor applicable security resources for updated requirements and compliance directions.
  • Up to 50% of responsibilities will be performed at Schriever Space Force Base. Travel to additional operational sites is required.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • Experience with implementation, deployment and sustainment of HBSS to include: ePO and Win/RHEL Point Products.
  • This ESS position requires security engineering skills with a working knowledge of Cybersecurity.
  • ESS administration experience on Win and RHEL systems is required.
  • Must have experience in security architecture design, security Audit tools, security requirements generation, security testing, and hardening workstations and servers.
  • Excellent technical document preparation skills and verbal communications are required for presentation of technical information assurance issues and reports to senior management.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • VM integration experience is highly desired.
  • Network design and software engineering backgrounds are a plus.
