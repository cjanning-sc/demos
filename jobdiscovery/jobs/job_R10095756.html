An organization within ACME Engineering’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Cutting Edge Optronics (CEO) is a leading provider of high-power laser diode arrays, high power pump modules and diode-pumped solid-state (DPSS) lasers. We are a vertically integrated manufacturer capable of supplying unmounted diode bars, packaged laser diodes, DPSS modules, laser diode drivers and complete DPSS laser systems. Our diode laser-based products have become industry standards and are used in a wide variety of commercial and military applications. 

  

Learn more about Cutting Edge Optronics at our website: https://www.acmeengineering.com/who-we-are/cutting-edge-optronics/. 

 

CEO seeks a highly motivated, seasoned Manufacturing Manager with strong team skills, able to lead an established manufacturing area to higher levels of productivity and performance through a significant production ramp-up period and beyond. The chosen candidate will display a passion for manufacturing results, have a proven track record of implementing significant manufacturing productivity improvements all while highly focused on team-oriented execution. 

 

Responsibilities 

 

  • Manage a manufacturing area with approximately 10-20 direct report hourly employees 

  • Accountability for achieving aggressive growth targets for production volume, on time delivery, quality focus and cost management 

  • Work closely with company-wide functions (Quality, Safety, Engineering, Customer Service, Finance, Procurement, Senior Management, etc.) to ensure all goals of manufacturing are accomplished with a focus on teamwork, collaboration, and regulatory compliance 

  • Manage productivity improvement initiatives using Six Sigma, Kaizen, Lean Manufacturing, etc. approaches 

  • Significant involvement with the implementation and improved utilization of an ERP system with focus on automation and efficiency improvements of volume manufacturing scheduling tools and techniques 

 

Basic Qualifications 

 

  • Bachelor’s degree with at least 5 years of applicable experience  

  • Experience managing direct reports in a manufacturing environment 

  • Demonstrated experience implementing Six Sigma, Kaizen, etc. productivity improvement initiatives 

  • Working knowledge of SPC utilization in a volume manufacturing environment 

  • Demonstrated experience with a relevant ERP/MRP system for volume manufacturing 

 

Preferred Qualifications  

 

  • Working knowledge of Visual Manufacturing 

  • Experience with manufacturing technician training systems and documentation 

  • Volume Manufacturing Management experience

  • Proven manufacturing capacity improvement

  • Manufacturing data analysis experience

  • Laser systems experience
