ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload & Ground Systems, Strategic Force Programs in Azusa, CA, is seeking an innovative Principal (Level 3) or /Sr. Principal (Level 4) Systems Engineer to support Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), system requirements and architectural modeling, and requirements and verification in support of the development of space payloads and ground systems. The organization supports space-based surveillance systems for varied missions including missile warning/defense and environmental monitoring. A qualified candidate will collaborate with diverse engineering teams across the product lifecycle from pre-proposal through product development, delivery, and sustainment to assist in understanding customer solution intent, establishing and managing the technical baseline, supporting system modeling, and developing content for review milestones. This role will report on-site daily. We can accommodate partial telework, but is dependent upon business/program need.

Additional responsibilities may be assigned commensurate with performance or due to business needs.

Basic Qualifications: (Principal Level 3)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years related technical experience in a Space/Defense industry position, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 3 years of related technical experience in a space/defense industry
  • Ability to obtain & maintain a DoD security clearance (US Citizenship Required)
  • SysML and Cameo expertise
  • Working knowledge of DOORS (Classic or Next Gen)
  • Experience/exposure to DODAF modeling

Basic Qualifications: (Sr. Principal Level 4)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 9 years related technical experience in a Space/Defense industry position, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 7 years of related technical experience in a space/defense industry
  • Ability to obtain & maintain a DoD security clearance (US Citizenship Required)
  • SysML and Cameo expertise
  • Working knowledge of DOORS (Classic or Next Gen)
  • Experience/exposure to DODAF modeling

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Certificate or advanced degree in Systems Engineering
  • Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 7 years (Sr. Principal Engineer) of experience in a Space/Defense industry position
  • Active DoD TS/SSBI clearance
  • Certificate in SysML and/or DODAF
  • DOORS experience including automation (DXL)
  • Experience with generating reports in Cameo utilizing VTL (Velocity Template Language)
  • Experience with generating customer-delivered DODAF views
  • Experience with related OMG schema such as the UML Testing Profile
  • Experience with modeling cyber-physical (hardware & software) solutions
  • Experience in an Agile modeling environment
