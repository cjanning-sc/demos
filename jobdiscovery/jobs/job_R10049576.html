Principal or Senior Principal, Vehicle Management Systems Engineer
R10049576
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is looking for a Principal or Senior Principal, Vehicle Management Systems Engineer to support the Systems Engineering organization in Redondo Beach, CA.

Position Overview:

The selected candidate will support research and development of flight controls for manned and unmanned air vehicles.  This is a dynamic and fun organization involved in deploying forward-leaning avionics, guidance and vehicle management systems for many of the newer concepts in development for air vehicles, weapons, and many more platforms.

Essential Duties: 

  • Perform hardware component trade studies.
  • Define hardware components and interfaces for flight critical system.
  • Develop electronics design coordinating with suppliers.
  • Implementation of Input / Output interfaces (i2c, serial, UDP/TCP, etc.).
  • Perform software development in C++.
  • Work in a Linux environment.
  • Deployment of software on real-time embedded hardware.
  • Develop and perform component, subsystem, and system level testing procedures to verify functionality.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications: 

  •  Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and at least 5 years of related engineering experience, or a Master's Degree and 3 years of experience, or 0 years of related engineering experience with a PhD.
  • Experience with C++ and Python for software development and scripting.
  • Demonstrated proficiency in MS Office with strong technical writing skills and the ability to communicate and present technical material to various stakeholders.
  • Active U.S. Government in-scope DoD Secret security clearance or higher is required, with the ability to obtain Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s Degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering discipline from an accredited university, and 7 years of related engineering experience
  • Experience with arm-based processors and FPGAs.
  • Experience with electronics design.
  • Proficient in C++ and Python, and development in Linux environment.
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).
