Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

SSSD has an opening for a Staff Program Integration Systems Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

This role will support the ARIS Portfolio Operations Director with logistics and oversight to enable program leadership to achieve operational excellence.

The candidate will be a key member of the Strategic Program Operations team which is responsible for technical planning, seamless integration and execution of execution and proposal activities across the portfolio.

The candidate will work directly with the Portfolio Operations Director, and Execution and Growth Program Offices to provide support to technical, schedule, and cost initiatives across execution and growth projects as well as proposal activities.

The candidate will work directly with the representatives from a range of customers to coordinate interactions at multiple levels of the organizations and ensure technical understanding of the critical items to be discussed during executive engagements.  Additionally, the candidate will work in support of the Affordability Campaign to drive down the cost and risk associated with program architecture and execution and drive initiatives through to completion.  Candidate will be required to perform analysis of technical, cost and schedule data in order to develop efficient solutions on a range of subjects in support of program execution. 

Successful candidates for this position should take a proactive approach to their work and do well with ambiguous scope, possess a high level of confidence, and feel comfortable contributing to discussions with executive level presence.

Basic Qualifications:

•  Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in a Business, Technical or related degree and with 15+ years of related professional experience in an operational role OR Master’s degree from an accredited university in a Business, Technical or related degree with 13+ years of related operational professional experience.

•  Experience with Windows-based MS Office tools, to include MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint.

•  Experience dealing with cost/schedule management and proposal execution.

•  Ability to concisely brief large forums.

•  Dynamic self-starter with strong attention to detail and the ability to function independently.
•  Demonstrated flexible approach to work and the ability to work collaboratively on a team.
•  Strong problem solving and analytical skills.
•  Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
•  Candidate must have, and be able to maintain, an active SCI clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

•  Prior experience as a resource analyst.
