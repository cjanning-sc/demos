We’re building something special here in Australia. Committed to safeguarding our country’s future by solving the toughest problems in Aeronautics, Space, and Mission Systems, we’re investing in our business, growing our capabilities, and nurturing a great reputation as the place to be in the Defence Industry. ‘Defining Possible’ is our mantra and we’re committed to making sure that’s as relevant for our people as it is for our customers.

Our Integrated Systems Operational Unit is at the forefront of emerging projects, products, systems and technologies which are paramount to ACME Engineering Australia’s growth strategy. This has opened up a new opportunity for a Project Scheduler to join our evolving and rapidly growing team.

Your role:

This is a critical role supporting our Program and Project Managers to achieve key objectives by leading the estimation, scheduling and management of new and existing projects ranging in size, complexity and capabilities.

Ideally you will have had similar experience in project-based organisations of large, multidisciplinary nature and be a confident communicator liaising with a variety of internal and external stakeholders.  Experience working with Earned Value Management principles and tools will be advantageous.  

This position can be based in Adelaide, Canberra or Brisbane.
