The Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Sr. Principal Systems Engineer (Mission Engineering) to join our team of qualified, diverse engineers on the E-2 Advanced Hawkeye Mission Engineering team. This position will be located in Melbourne, FL.

Essential Functions:
• Key aspects include organization and self-drive to gather required information, work with stakeholders, and provide analyses and presentations. 
• Performs mission engineering activities to ensure continuity between desired operational capabilities and delivered system performance, through the Systems Engineering “V”. 
• Ensures performance capabilities of new systems and architectures are traded against cost and mission requirements; develops mission relevant scenarios; assesses operational effectiveness of existing and emerging systems and capabilities in representative operational environments.
• Develops new and/or integrates existing system simulation frameworks, performance models and algorithms, threat models, and command and control models.
• Simulates real-time operations and develops software that represent projected behavior of systems.
• Develops, integrates, and uses advanced graphical user interfaces and visualization tools.
• Models operational environments, performs trade studies via computer simulation and recommends alternative architectures.
• Develops and adapts existing models, methods, and simulations to support the design, development, and sustainment of system design concepts and technologies. 
• Performs trade studies and simulation analyses with the objective of enhancing and optimizing system performance capabilities.
• Incorporates AI/ML techniques in Modeling and Simulation frameworks.
• Solicits input from various stakeholders, synthesizes data, and communicates results to various levels of leadership within the organization.
• This position requires practical experience and interpretation of modeling and simulation results, algorithms, and formulas and must be able to communicate their significance and relevancy in a clear, detailed manner.

Basic Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s Degree and 9 years of experience; Master’s Degree and 7 years of experience; Ph.D and 4 years of experience
• Proficiency in Python and MATLAB developing tools and algorithms
• Experience developing and integrating Target Tracking, Multiple Hypothesis Tracking, and/or Data Fusion algorithms
• Proven experience leading projects with multiple teams and managing relationships with both internal and external customers
• Ability to communicate highly technical content, findings and recommendations clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences
• Must have an Active, In-Scope, DOD Secret Security Clearance or higher (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
• Must be able to obtain and maintain Special Program Access as required per business requirements

Preferred Qualifications:
• Previous experience in aviation and defense programs 
• Experience with Link-16 correlation and interoperability
• Proven ability to discover tasks that can be automated for efficiency
• Experience with system of systems modeling tools
• Experience with AGILE, MBSE, and/or system architecture
• Effective project management skills and good communication techniques.

• Strong interpersonal and leadership skills.
• Excellent communications skills, including the ability to present and brief technical data to all levels of leadership within the organization and U.S. Government.
• A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Top Secret security clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (SAP)
