C2BMC Software Engineer/Principal Software Engineer

ACME Engineering Space Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Software Engineer/Principal Software Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Huntsville, AL.

Position Overview:

  

The selected Software Engineer / Principal Software Engineer will be responsible for software development for the C2BMC BM/SRM Product Team, performing analysis and technical support of systems software products, including resolution, design, development, testing, operational integration, and user support.

Essential Functions:  

  • Support advanced portions of the lifecycle of software development to include requirements definition, integration, and deployment.
  • Prioritize and adjust tasks to accomplish project results with limited oversight and direction.
  • Apply "systems thinking" to ensure developed products are scalable, maintainable, and meet end-user needs.
  • Communicate openly and effectively with teammates and customer representatives.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Software Engineer or a Principal Software Engineer level.

BQs for a Software Engineer are:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 2 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline and 0 years of related experience.
  • Good working knowledge of MS Office products.
  • Experience developing software in object-oriented programming languages such as C, C++, or Java.
  • Knowledge of basic software practices such as coding standards and configuration management
  • Experience in the use of common software development and management tools such as Git, JIRA and Eclipse.
  • Ability and willingness to travel 10% to support business needs.
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance.

BQs for a Principal Software Engineer are:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline preferred from an accredited university and 5 years of related experience, or a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline and 3 years of related experience, or 0 years of experience with a PhD.
  • Good working knowledge of MS Office products.
  • Experience developing software in object-oriented programming languages such as C, C++, or Java.
  • Knowledge of basic software practices such as coding standards and configuration management
  • Experience in the use of common software development and management tools such as Git, JIRA and Eclipse.
  • Ability and willingness to travel 10% to support business needs.
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Strong interpersonal skills to collaborate effectively with technical teams and customer personnel.
  • Experience analyzing complex problems and developing solutions based on limited direction and definition.
  • Experience developing Enterprise applications.
  • Familiarity with Java foundation classes and toolkits.
  • Knowledge of server side development and computer system administration.
  • Flexibility and willingness to take on unanticipated tasking.
  • A current active U.S. Government DoD Top Secret security clearance.
