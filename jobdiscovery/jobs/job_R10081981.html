Principal Systems Engineer/Architect – Top Secret (Satellite/Space Systems)
R10081981
1st Shift (United States of America)

Successful candidates for this position will work in a technical role in Dulles VA.  This Systems Engineering role is responsible for mission onboarding efforts for a space program in the National Security Space business area within the Space Systems Sector.

The Mission Onboarding Engineer will perform mission onboarding activities for a ground based service oriented architecture intended to service next generation space capability.  The Mission Onboarding Engineer will develop critical mission operations products in support of orbital analysis to include identification and integration of signature limitations and constraints.  A successful Mission Onboarding Engineer will have experience creating mission user guides, providing mission liaison support to mission operations, and integrating software systems from different mission segments.

This position will expose the candidate to equal parts technical competency, forward thinking ideas, and execution focus. This position requires a functional understanding of ground system networking, private cloud deployments, data and messaging buses, software deployment, and hardware deployment. This position utilizes abilities to interact in an organized and solutions-oriented manner, with external entities who are contributing elements of the integrated ground segment.

The candidate will work with cross-discipline team members and must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work with a diverse community of engineers and managers to achieve the goals of the program team.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top Secret level clearance, SCI eligible.
  • BS degree in Engineering or related technical field and at least 5 years of applicable experience, or an MS Degree and 3 years of applicable experience

Preferred Experience (one or more areas desired):

  • Experience with Satellite Ground Systems in the US National Security arena
  • CONOPS development for Space Based programs.
  • Knowledge of various mission analysis techniques and software tools.
  • Space to Ground and Mission Architecture
  • Understanding of Agile software development practices.
  • Background working software systems engineering.
  • On-orbit testing of space systems and testing of integrated ground systems.
