ACME Engineering's Navigation, Targeting, & Survivability Division is seeking a motivated systems engineer at the Woodland Hills, CA location to support the development and production of inertial, radio, magnetic, and vision aided navigation systems.

A systems engineer in this business area is responsible for working with a broad customer base to identify unique requirements, flow the requirements into design documentation, and work with a diverse team to build and test those navigation systems.  The systems engineer is the technical lead and point of contact to the customer. Expected duties include:

  • Presenting to customers on the capabilities of current products, performance analyses, design information, and requirements verification methods.
  • Generating, reviewing, and managing multiple requirement sets that cover all aspects of both performance and non-performance including mechanical interfaces, electrical interfaces, data requirements, environmental requirements, and reliability.
  • Awareness and responsiveness to contract milestones and deliverables.
  • Leading a team of subsystem engineers and subject matter experts to ensure the designs are sufficient to meet the system requirements.
  • Working with integration and test engineers to ensure all requirements are verified through inspection, analysis, demonstration, or test often by providing test and data reduction scripts.
  • Providing test direction to ensure production tests and troubleshooting tests are executed correctly and resulting data conforms to requirements.
  • Analyzing performance information for navigation sensors against their error budget
  • Generating control systems that integrate multiple navigation sensor data through kalman filters.
  • Develop software and embedded systems requirements.
  • Model the expected performance of navigation systems and their sensors utilizing covariance and monte carlo analysis.
