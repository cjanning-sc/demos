ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Risk Management Analyst. This position will be located in Roy, UT or may be filled virtually based on most qualified candidate.

What you will get to do:

specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

Acting as an IPT Risk Manager, oversee and manage the operational aspects of Risk, Issue, and Opportunity (RIO) Management. Integrate with the Integrated Product Team (IPT) to help Identify RIO items within their Statement of Work (SOW) responsibility, including any potential Intercompany Work Orders (IWOs) and Suppliers.  Act as a liaison to ensure high quality inputs and adherence to relative program toolsets and processes. Support the IPT in all levels of RIO Boards including Program Management and Customer Reviews. Review status of projects and budgets; manage schedules and prepare status reports. Assess project issues and develop resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives. Develop mechanisms for managing and monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with project managers, IPTs, and functional management.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.  

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree plus 15 years of experience; 13 with Masters in Business Management, Industrial Engineering, or Leading Teams. 4 additional years of relevant experience in lieu of degree may be considered.

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain a Secret level clearance.

  • Experience supporting DOD Programs.

  • Experience presenting to upper-level management.

  • Proficient on MS Excel, PowerPoint, and Word.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experienced in Risk Management Processes and Toolsets.

  • PMP Certification.

  • Background in Leading teams.

  • Currently GBSD Accessed.

  • Current DoD Top Secret Clearance.
