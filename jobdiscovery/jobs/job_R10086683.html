ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Data Scientist to join the Program Integration Team (PIT).   This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  Telecommuting opportunities may exist.

What You’ll Get To Do:

Data scientist / Data analytics professional responsible for production of analytics dashboards using Tableau

The candidate is responsible for the followings

  • Design develop visualizations to assist management in decision-making
  • Understand Data preparation /  cleaning and integrate various data sources
  • Understand and apply basic UX/UI fundamentals to improve user experience and consumption of large amounts of information from multiple datasets
  • Understand how to apply different visualization concepts (e.g. scatter plot, S-chart, heat map, etc.)  and their applications in analytics reports

Understand design, develop scalable ETL pipeline processes & technologies
