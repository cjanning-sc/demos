ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Cyber Systems Engineer OR Principal Cyber Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, FL.

This position is for a Lab Operations Cyber Systems Engineer in Melbourne, Florida. This position will entail maintaining smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators. Interacting with users and evaluating vendor products. Making recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinating installation and providing backup recovery. Develops and monitors policies and standards for allocation related to the use of computing resources. Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.  Lab Operations Cyber supports multiple shifts.  Training will occur on first shift; however, successful candidate may be placed on second shift or weekend shift (alternate shifts include pay differential) additional compensation applies to alternate shifts.

**This position may be filled by either level based on the qualifications listed.**

Essential Functions

  • Support operation and maintenance of Network/Security Operations Center (NOC/SOC)
  • Maintain availability of Test Station and Software Development Environment 
  • Ensure systems adhere to program configuration and data management plans
  • Ensure Lab Operations Policy and Procedures as being executed and updated
  • Establish strict program control processes to ensure mitigation of risks and support certification and accreditation of systems
  • Assist with system audits and monitor corrective actions

Basic Qualifications:

For – Cyber Systems Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 2 years of cyber and/or systems administration experience, a Master’s Degree and 0 years of experience, an Associate’s degree and 4 years of experience, or in lieu of degree 6 years of experience may be considered
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance prior to commencement of employment
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to commencement of employment

Basic Qualifications:

For - Principal Cyber Systems Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 5+ years of cyber and/or systems administration experience, a Master’s Degree and 3+ years, an Associate’s degree and 7 years of experience, or in lieu of degree 9+ years of experience may be considered  
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance prior to commencement of employment
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to commencement of employment

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD secret clearance
  • Systems Administration experience, to include Windows, Linux, and VM environments
  • DoD 8570 Level 2 Certification, such as Security+ CE 
  • Cybersecurity/Information Assurance experience
  • Understanding of enterprise-level computing platforms involving virtualization, distribution and high availability
  • Experience implementing storage allocation, reclamation, replication and migration
  • Experience operating and configuring security relevant tools such as Splunk and Nessus
