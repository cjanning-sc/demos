ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Project Management 3 or Project Managment 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located onsite in Melbourne, FL.

Essential Functions:

This position is seeking a highly motivated, strong communicator to join the Global Supply Chain Program Management (GSCPM) – Integration & Affordability team. This position will be in fast-paced, classified environment supporting a bi-coastal team.

Tasks include:

Integration:

Establishes rhythms, processes, and tool aids to synchronize Global Supply Chain (GSC) functions across enterprise and division. 

Presents to enterprise leadership on program and business status. Manage up and out and prepare business stakeholders for program meetings. 

Drive dashboard creation using program tools, including Tableau

Affordability

Coordinate with business stakeholders to identify and implement potential initiatives. Communicate plan and status to program leadership through classified and unclassified data calls.

Baseline, Monitor/Control and Reporting

Manages material and/or labor control portfolios related to the GSC Statement of work

Provide the progress of work against the schedule and update forecast based in conjunction with cost planners and business stakeholders.

Review sales forecast with business stakeholders and implement changes. Communicate updates and status on monthly basis to leadership. 

Identify scope changes and coordinate budget and schedule impacts with business management as well as business stakeholders via Change Requests. Support program development of Change Board packages. 

Analyze and report significant cost and schedule variances to business stakeholders. Develop and complete the variance analysis report (VAR) and close outstanding action items.

RIO 

Facilitate and aid development of major stakeholders in Risk, Issue and Opportunity and bring to the program. Exercise and track mitigation steps to close actions. 

Change Execution

Review change board packages and assess for potential impacts

Coordinate with Cost Management to process budget/scope and/or EAC EVCR

Track implementation of scope change to closure

Report and accuracy monthly to program and GSC leadership

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 10 years of business experience or Master's Degree with 8 years of business experience (12 years of business experience in lieu of a degree). Business experience shall be in supply chain management, logistics, subcontracts management, contracts management, proposal price/cost analysis, and/or strategic sourcing.
  • Must have an active Secret DoD Clearance
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access
  • Intermediate MS Excel Skills
  • Intermediate SAP Skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Aerospace background
  • Supply chain, cost mgmt. or CAMs
  • SAP
  • CAM Certified
  • MS office Suite knowledge

We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.
