ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Test Data Analysis Lead Engineer.  This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel – Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers and other engineers. 

The Data Analysts will develop analysis and reporting capabilities. They will also monitor performance and quality control plans to identify improvements. 

Experience working with data generated from automated SW test Systems.

Responsibilities include:

•Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports

•Develop and implement data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality

•Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems

•Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets

•Work with management to prioritize test data analysis and use for design improvements
