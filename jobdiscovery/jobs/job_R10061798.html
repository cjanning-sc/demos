ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Software Configuration Analyst as the Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Subject Matter Expert (SME). This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

Roles and responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

• Configuration Management administration of PLM (Teamcenter) and CAD Data Management.

• End-user technical support of PLM software in development and production environments.

• Performing root cause analysis and debugging problems within the PLM environment, and working with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence.

• Monitoring, addressing, and resolving production PLM environment incidents, workflows, client install packages collaboratively with other support teams.

• Documenting all aspects of system support so that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting PLM instances.

• Supporting UAT with business users as necessary.

• Supporting business readiness and change management activities prior to go-live and in production.

Job Qualifications:

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

• Bachelor’s degree with 14 years of experience. 

• 6+ years PLM support experience with Teamcenter.

• Proven track record supporting major Teamcenter software in an enterprise environment.

• Experience with Teamcenter support and incident resolution.

• Current Comptia Security+, maintaining a current Security+CE certification will be a condition of continued employment.

Must be a US Citizen, with an active Top Secret Clearance and eligible for Special Program Access (SAP)

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

• Familiarity with the functions, processes, and capabilities in Teamcenter and CAD Data Management.

• Fluency in at least one computer programming language.

• Ability to apply engineering techniques and analytical methods to make recommendations for improvements and provide information and assistance to end-users.

• Familiarity with AWS Cloud.
