ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Sector Strategic Sourcing Engineer Level 4 or Level 5 to join our Sector Strategic Sourcing Team. This position can be located in Baltimore, MD, Dulles, VA, Roy, UT, Redondo Beach, CA or Chandler, AZ. Part time remote work may be available.  

Position Summary/Responsibilities:

The Sector Strategic Sourcing Engineer role within the Sector Strategic Sourcing organization is a collaborative role responsible for the development and implementation of advanced supply chain strategies in support of technology maturation, operational deployment and program performance.  As a senior technical GSC leadership position, the role will focus on identifying and developing supplier capabilities aligned to NGSP technology roadmaps, collaborating with CTO's office on technology initiatives including identifying partnership opportunities with the supply base, developing and implementing processes for identification and illustration of supplier capabilities, and pursuing additional supply base identification and expansion/sourcing projects.  This position will interface with many of the different NGSP organizations as well as provide engagement opportunities with enterprise sectors, corporate staff and senior level leadership.

Responsibilities include:

  • Lead source and capability development projects, while ensuring alignment for technology source development to CTO roadmap

  • In collaboration with Strategy/BD and technical leaders throughout the sector, develop methods to determine future capability/technology demands from supply base to support planned growth and investments needed for success

  • Analysis of key emerging technologies’ supply base and industry analysis

  • Identify opportunities for and support the development of strategic collaboration agreements

  • Lead projects to frame, develop and deliver solutions to meet the sector’s long term strategic goals.

  • Support special projects within Sector Strategic Sourcing Team, as needed

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors' Degree in STEM

  • US Citizenship and ability to obtain government security clearance

  • Minimum of 9 years demonstrated success/problem solving skills in Supply Chain, Operations, Logistics, Engineering or Program Management; 7 years with a Master’s for a level 4

  • Minimum of 14 years demonstrated success/problem solving skills in Supply Chain, Operations, Logistics, Engineering or Program Management; 12 years with a Master’s for a level 5

  • Experience with and a working knowledge of Space Systems product lines and technology building blocks.

  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite

  • Demonstrated technology expertise and leadership success, and ability to influence and interact with peers and all levels of management in the organization

  • Highly effective interpersonal skills, commitment to fostering an engaging environment for others and excellent communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with technology roadmaps and/or high-level, complex technical process understanding

  • Experience with sourcing activities, including source selection, high-level capacity and capability analysis and alternate sourcing

  • Demonstrated working knowledge of data analytic applications and techniques and methods for strategy development and deployment.

  • Experience with the use of agile methods for project planning and execution.
