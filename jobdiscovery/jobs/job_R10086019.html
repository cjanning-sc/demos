SSSD has an opening for a Principal/Sr. Principal Risk and Affordability Engineer to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA. 

This position will support the Assured Interoperable Missions (AIM) Business Unit as the Risk Lead and will be responsible for the following: 

•  Maintaining the program risk database
•  Facilitating cross coordination between Integrated Product Teams (IPTs) with Risk and Opportunity Working Groups
•  Delivering monthly Contract Data Requirements Lists (CDRLs) to the customer and interfacing with the program business team
•  Ensuring program risks are accurately tracked and mitigated
•  Running and coordinating the program Risk and Opportunity Management Board

This role will also support the affordability efforts of the program, by partnering with Program Management and other Program Leadership to support affordability initiatives and schedule execution across in-house and subcontracted activities. This role will report to the program Director of Production and Affordability. Specific responsibilities of this role include assisting with the following: 

•  Coordination of weekly and monthly production rhythm meetings
•  Support of end-to-end production planning and efficiency efforts
•  Management of cost and schedule reduction opportunities

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Risk and Affordability Engineer or Sr. Principal Risk and Affordability Engineer level based on the qualifications listed below. 

Basic Qualifications - Principal Risk and Affordability Engineer

•   Bachelor's degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) discipline with 5+ years of engineering experience OR Master's degree in STEM discipline with 3+ years of engineering experience OR PhD in STEM discipline
•  Candidate must have, and be able to maintain an active U.S. Government security clearance - Dept. of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years 

Basic Qualifications - Sr. Principal Risk and Affordability Engineer

•  Bachelor's degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) discipline with 9+ years of engineering experience OR Master's degree in STEM discipline with 7+ years of engineering experience OR PhD in STEM discipline with 4+ years of engineering experience
•  Candidate must have, and be able to maintain an active U.S. Government security clearance - Dept. of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years 

Preferred Qualifications: 

•  Experienced in Risk Management Processes and Toolsets 
•  Experience with Affordability/Cost Reduction Initiatives 
•  Background in leading teams
