ACME Engineering has an opening for either a Principal Proposal (Pricing) Analyst or a Sr Principal Proposal (Pricing) Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located on site in Redondo Beach, CA with partial telework available.

Essential Functions:

  • Develops and helps lead proposals by providing clear pricing/estimating direction from proposal kick-off and continued guidance throughout the process for successful proposal execution
  • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments
  • Reviews and guides the functions in developing quality basis of estimates
  • Responsible for preparing timely, accurate and compliant price proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications, FAR / DFARS requirements and Sector Estimating System and Procedures
  • Researches and analyzes historical data and develops pricing models to support proposal efforts
  • Possesses the ability to display quantitative data (pricing) in a simple, intuitive format and to present findings in a clear and concise manner to management / leadership team
  • Effectively supports customer fact-finding and Leads DCAA / DCMA audits
  • Participates as an active member of the contract negotiating team
  • Provides day-to-day direction and guidance to junior pricing analysts
  • Will be required to manage a diverse portfolio of pricing activities including, but not limited to, Proposal Cost Volumes, Basis of Estimates, Cost Estimating Factors and ad-hoc analysis
  • Develop reports and tools as needed to support analytical needs of the pricing effort
  • Initiates process improvements to enhance productivity & efficiency within Pricing & Estimating Â 
  • Delivers innovative, flexible, integrated solutions to meet customers changing business needs

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Proposal Analyst level or a Sr Principal Proposal Analyst level.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Proposal (Pricing) Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 6 years of pricing/estimating, finance, contracts, proposals, and/or business-related experience, or a Master's degree with 4 years of stated experience
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)

Basic Qualifications for Sr Principal Proposal (Pricing) Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 10 years of pricing/estimating, finance, contracts, proposals, and/or business-related experience, or a Master's degree with 8 year of stated experience
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Clearance
  • Advanced Excel skills (write complex formulas, produce live models, code VBA, etc)
  • Knowledge of the government regulations FAR and DFARS
  • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills
  • Knowledge of Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) and accounting/proposal auditing techniques
