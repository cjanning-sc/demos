Principal Electronics Engineer/ Sr. Principal Electronics Engineer
R10063440
1st Shift (United States of America)

NGMS is seeking an Electronics Engineer with the desire to learn new technologies to join our innovative Test Design Solutions (TDS) team to help develop, enhance and maintain the Electrical Ground Support Equipment (EGSE) suite of test sets. Join a design community that offers unique design challenges that combine the talents of multiple engineering disciplines including Hardware, Firmware, Software, Mechanical Design, Electrical Design, Systems Engineering and Program Management.

Candidate responsibilities will include but not limited to panel & cable design, electrical interconnects & schematics, developing acceptance criteria & certification procedures, and hardware checkout/troubleshooting. Root cause determination, problem resolution and documentation is expected. In this role, the candidate will interface with subsystem test engineers, test operators, technicians, systems administrators, program managers, security team, and vendors in order to provide support for various test set tasks as required. Position focuses on the full design cycle of EGSE, from conception through design to fabrication and integration/test. Position may require some travel.  This position will be located in Linthicum, MD, within the Test Design Solutions Department.

Basic Qualifications Principal Electrical Engineer

  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering or related STEM discipline and 5 years’ experience with a Bachelor’s Degree; 3 years with Masters

  • Experience with Electrical designs such as integrated (analog, power, digital), panel design, cable design, and schematic layouts.

  • Work effectively in a team environment

  • Communicate effectively both orally and in writing

  • Work independently with minimal Management Oversight 

  • Ability to obtain secret clearance

Basic Qualifications Sr. Principal Electrical Engineer

  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering or related STEM discipline and 9 years’ experience with a Bachelor’s Degree; 7 years with Masters; 4 years with PhD

  • Experience with Electrical designs such as integrated (analog, power, digital), panel design, cable design, and schematic layouts.

  • Work effectively in a team environment

  • Communicate effectively both orally and in writing

  • Work independently with minimal Management Oversight 

  • Lead a team of engineers

  • Ability to obtain secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Electrical Engineering or related STEM discipline

  • Experience with electrical design tools (e.g., Zuken, AutoCAD, PSpice)

  • Experience with hands-on troubleshooting with electrical hardware

  • Experience with Programmable Automation Controllers  

  • Experience with using standard test equipment, including oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, protocol analyzers, pattern generators, and spectrum analyzers

  • Active or Current Secret Clearance or higher is preferred
