ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has multiple openings for Structural Design Engineers or Principal Structural Design Engineers to join our team of qualified diverse individuals. In Vehicle Engineering, we develop manned and unmanned aircraft platforms for all programs in the Aeronautics Systems Sector.

These positions will be located in Palmdale, CA and have the possibility to work multiple shifts:

  • 9/80 work scheduled (week 1, Monday - Friday, week 2, Monday - Thursday Friday off)
  • Monday - Thursday on a 4 day - 10 hours/day schedule. Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed
  • Friday, Saturday, Sunday on a 3 day - 10 hours/day schedule (you will be paid for a 40-hour work week). Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed

We are looking for Engineers to develop “design for producibility” solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In the Structural Design group, we develop solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In your day-to-day activities you will participate in the development and review of completed models, drawings, and repairs for accuracy, clarity, and completeness, in order to provide evaluation of engineering products for conformity to standards, procedures, and specifications. You will also supervise design solutions from initial concept to final implementation and give concise presentations and explanations of structural issues/solutions to internal management and customer representatives.

These positions may be filled at either the Structural Design Engineer or Principal Structural Design Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and 2 years of experience with a background in structural design OR a Master’s Degree in the fields listed above with 0 years of relevant experience.
  • Experience in the design of bonded composite primary and secondary aircraft structures.
  • High level of proficiency in a 3D CAD related software in the development of aircraft structures and components.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain an active DoD Secret clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to commencement of employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained prior to commencement of employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and 2 years of experience with a background in structural design OR a Master’s Degree in the fields listed above with 0 years of relevant experience OR a PhD in the fields listed above with 0 years of relevant experience.
  • Experience in the design of bonded composite primary and secondary aircraft structures.
  • High level of proficiency in a 3D CAD related software in the development of aircraft structures and components.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain an active DoD Secret clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to commencement of employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained prior to commencement of employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications for both levels:

  • Active DoD Secret or Top Secret clearance
  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Aerospace Engineering
  • Experience using Unigraphics Nx or CATIA V5
  • Experience working on Special Access Programs
  • Experience working on military aircraft
  • Experience with durability and damage tolerance (DaDT) of aircraft structures
