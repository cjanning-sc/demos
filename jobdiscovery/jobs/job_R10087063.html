What you will get to do:

The ACME Engineering Specialized Warfighter Development Contract (SWDC) Modeling and Simulation Software (MSS) is seeking Software Engineering Manager to join our team of qualified and diverse leaders at Schriever Space Force Base in Colorado Springs, Colorado.  The SWDC program provides many key capabilities to the Missile Defense Agency to include advanced OPIR tracking capability, ground test support, and key modeling and simulation capabilities.  The software engineering manager is responsible for supervising a team of engineers, hiring engineers, and performance evaluation. The software engineering manager will be responsible for ensuring compliance to software engineering standards, and best practices. In this role they will work closely with program to provide guidance on software development tools and provide guidance to the team’s use of the software development tools. In summary, the software engineering manager will

  • Have responsibility for designing, developing, testing, integrating, documenting and releasing M&S software applications
  • Lead a team of approximately 25 software engineers and provide guidance through the software engineering lifecycle to include architectural decision making
  • Lead the effort of modernizing existing software baseline
  • Interface with program management, system engineering and customer stakeholders on matters relating to software engineering products
  • Ensure engineering team has necessary resources and equipment to perform job
  • Provide regular employee engagement such as regular 1:1s, and career development
  • Work with program stakeholders to understand program engineering needs and perform hiring responsibilities in accordance with needs
  • Position will require up to 25% travel.

Basic Qualifications

  • 9 years with BS; 7 years with Masters; 5 years with PhD.
  • Experience with Agile principles to include Kanban, Scrum.
  • Experience managing small to medium sized teams.
  • Knowledge of DevOps tools to include Continuous Integration and Unit Test frameworks.
  • Virtual machine, and containerization familiarization
  • Position requires U.S. Citizenship
  • Must have a DoD Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with Missile Defense Agency Modeling and Simulation products.
  • Familiarity with Missile Defense System Architecture and Mission.
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills.
