ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Grounds Systems Engineer/Sr. Principal Grounds Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Oklahoma City, OK.

**This position may be filled by either a Principal Grounds Systems Engineer level or a Sr. Principal Grounds Systems Engineer level based on the qualifications listed.

Essential Functions:

•            Experience with DevOps environments, virtual cloud solutions/architectures

•            Strong Customer interaction Skills, problem solving  and critical thinking at Customer facility

•            Ability to brief program leadership and resolve major IT roadblocks to facilitate Team success

•            Experience with Agile concepts and framework

•            Willing to travel up to 50% of time

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

For Principal Grounds Systems Engineer (T03):

•            Must have a Bachelor’s degree in a STEM discipline AND 5 years of related professional/military software and/or systems engineering experience, OR a Master’s degree AND 3 years of related professional/military software and/or systems engineering experience, or a Ph.D.

•            Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (or higher) that is in-scope (granted or renewed within the last 6 years).

•            Must be able to attain and maintain Special Program Access (PAR/SAP) prior to start

•            Must have demonstrated experience in Cloud based networking design, to include IP address planning, switching, and routing

Basic Qualifications:

For Sr. Principal Grounds Systems Engineer (T04):

•            Must have a Bachelor’s degree in a STEM discipline AND 9 years of related professional/military software and/or systems engineering experience, OR a Master’s degree AND 7 years of related professional/military software and/or systems engineering experience, or a Ph.D AND 4 years of related professional/military software and/or systems engineering experience.

•            Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (or higher) that is in-scope (granted or renewed within the last 6 years).

•            Must be able to attain and maintain Special Program Access (PAR/SAP) prior to start

•            Must have demonstrated experience in Cloud based networking design, to include IP address planning, switching, and routing

Preferred Qualifications:

•            DOD Secret or Top Secret/SCI level Security Clearance, to include a Single Scope Background Investigation, a periodic reinvestigation, or an original adjudication date completed within the last 6 years

•            Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD Degree in Electronics, Computer or Electrical Engineering

•            Experience with working on a multidisciplinary team and writing technical proposals

•            Possess a DODD 8570 certifications, such as CompTIA Security+, CISSP ,Security +

•            Working knowledge of cyber security, STIGs and associated elements

•            Ground system setup, software development and/or systems administration
