Electronics Engineer - Communications Hardware Engineer
R10035885
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a successful candidate to fill a Hardware Engineering role within the Communications IPT. Responsible for working with an integrated product team (IPT) structure to perform engineering capture, build, integration, test and maintenance of communications hardware in laboratory and field settings. This includes the specification and usage of specialized test and support equipment.


The position is located in Palmdale, CA and reports directly to the Communications Hardware Lead, with responsibilities including (but not limited to) the following:

• Install communication, test and support equipment in lab and field environments.
• Perform electrical and mechanical (signal, power & thermal) analysis at the rack level.
• Generation of engineering drawings in accordance with ASME and IEEE standards, including rack assembly drawings, interconnect wiring diagrams and custom cable assemblies.
• Analysis of supplier LRUs, including step-file and 3D model review and compliance to environmental specifications.
• This position requires supporting system integration, test, development and execution of various types of complex communications systems.
• Specification of rack cabling (RF, optical, 1553, power) and cooling systems.
• Provide lab and site integration, test and maintenance support.
• Participate in troubleshooting systems to resolve complex issues, interact and coordinate with responsible communications engineers responsible for sustaining communication sub-systems.
• Contribute to improvement of processes, design and future sustainment efforts.

 Basic Qualifications Electronics Engineer:
• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 2 years of experience or Master’s Degree in STEM with 0 years of experience.
• Active DoD Top Secret clearance with investigation date within the last 5 years and ability to obtain and maintain access to Special Programs (SAP).
• Ability to travel up to 25%.
• Must have high attention to detail and emphasis on first time quality.
• Must be physically able to move up to 50 lbs.
• Must have color vision to identify cables and wires.
• Candidate must have strong communication and interpersonal skills with an aptitude to learn new material quickly.
• Working knowledge of governing and industry standards to include MIL-STD-1553, MIL-STD-1760, MIL-STD-100G, and ASME Y14.100.
• Must be a self-directed technical performer with demonstrated experience leading multi-disciplinary technical trades, and ability to develop and execute to a strategy.

Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience with drawing and release tools – (CATIA V5, Visio, Teamcenter Enterprise)
• Working knowledge of various communication protocols including Ethernet, 1553 bus systems and FibreChannel.
• Working knowledge of communication engineering theory and RF systems.
