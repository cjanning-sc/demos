Principal Mechanical Design Engineer 3 / Sr Principal Mechanical Design Engineer 4 - Tactical Space Systems - Gilbert, AZ
R10094950
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Tactical Space Systems Group is seeking a mid-level to senior-level Mechanical Designer Engineer to perform mechanical design tasks on one or more spacecraft systems or subsystems.

The Mechanical Designer will work with other designers and engineers on Spacecraft Structures, Subsystems, Mechanisms, MGSE, or Proposals across the three business areas to develop and enhance critical technical capabilities that drive our businesses.

Skills include, but not limited to, Computer Aided Design (CAD), knowledge of Geometric Dimension and Tolerancing (GD&T), Engineering Drawings, and Design for Manufacturing.

Essential Functions:

  • Work on all Spacecraft Structures, Payload, and Antenna mechanical hardware from requirements all the way through integration and test.

  • Perform detailed design tasks using CreoParametric Computer Aided Design (CAD) and other software tools to develop and document designs for manufacturing and ultimately assembly into spacecraft.

  • Produce Mechanical drawings using industry standard GD&T processes.

  • Interface with Mechanical design and engineering team to produce efficient and manufacturable spacecraft parts and assemblies.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Mechanical Design Engineer 3 level or Sr. Principal Mechanical Design Engineer 4.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Mechanical Designer 3:

  • 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD.

  • Experience with performing mechanical tasks associated with design and manufacturing of structures.

  • Experience with CreoParametric Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.

  • Be eligible to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Mechanical Designer 4:

  • 9 Years with Bachelors in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD.

  • Experience with performing mechanical tasks associated with design and manufacturing of structures.

  • Experience with CreoParametric Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.

  • Be eligible to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with the fundamentals of spacecraft layout and design.

  • Experience with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) standards and preparation of manufacturing documentation.

  • Experience with 3D Printing, Design for Manufacturing, and hands-on hardware fabrication.

  • Proficiency in Microsoft office suite.
