ACME Engineering Mission Systems has an opening for a Sr. Principal Supply Chain Planner to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals supporting the F-35 portfolio. This position specifically focuses on proposal support to win new business under the F-35 CNI Development, Production and Sustainment Operating Units.

The candidate must be able to function in a dynamic and fast paced proposal environment and work closely with proposal teams, material estimators, price/cost analysts, subcontracts and procurement personnel to ensure the vendor data is compliant in support of the ACME Engineering proposal submittal. The role is both strategic and administrative in nature.

We are well-known for our inclusive, family environment, as well as our excellent work/life balance. We also offer exceptional benefits/healthcare, a 9/80 schedule, and a great 401K matching program. The position is located in San Diego, California. Remote or hybrid work can be considered.

Duties:

  • Develops, plans and schedules support to key proposal milestones through submittal
  • Compiles cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications
  • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments
  • Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts
  • Review of Request for Proposal (RFP) requirements
  • Working collaboratively with the Program Office, Pricing and Supply Chain during the proposal phase of a contract to ensure ACME Engineering is providing the best supplier value for the customer.
  • Develops the Basis of Estimates (BOE’s) for subcontract Hardware, IWOs and GSC labor compiling the required supporting data to ensure compliance with FAR and NG policies and procedures for U.S. and International proposals.
  • Participates in customer fact-findings, negotiations and DCAA/DCMA Audits as required
  • The successful candidate will possess excellent interpersonal skills to interface with suppliers, customers and all levels of internal management and functional staff.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with 10+ years of experience in Proposals, Pricing and Estimating, Business Management, Subcontracts, Supply Chain Management and/or Contract Management or Master's degree with 8+ years of related experience
  • DoD contracting experience (e.g. FAR and DFARS government regulations)
  • Knowledge and understanding of labor/material estimating, proposal analysis, and proposal adequacy as prescribed in DFARS
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience conducting cost analysis
  • Demonstrated project management skills
  • Project Management Certification
  • Demonstrated leadership skills
  • Ability to present information in front of senior management
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Ability to effectively manage competing priorities and deadlines
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills
