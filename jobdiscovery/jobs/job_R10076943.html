ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an Associate Systems Architect/Systems Architect to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida.

The Associate Systems Architect/Systems Architect will work to perform functional analysis and Model-based Systems Engineering (MBSE) processes for incremental capability development and integration on military aircraft platforms in a fast-paced environment. This individual will collaborate with several stakeholders across distributed teams (subsystem developers, system integrators, system testers, etc.) to support delivery of quality design products.  This individual will maintain technical cognizance of subsystem interdependencies and maturity of interfaces for identifying and resolving gaps.

Basic Qualifications for an Associate Systems Architect:
• Bachelor’s degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline from an accredited university
• Experience with Systems Engineering principles and products

• Ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret or Top-Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Systems Architect:

• Bachelor’s degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline from an accredited university and 2 years of experience in systems engineering OR Master’s degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline from an accredited university and 0 years of experience in systems engineering
• Experience with Systems Engineering principles and products

• Ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret or Top-Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:
• An active, In-scope DoD Secret or Top-Secret clearance
•  Previous or existing Program Access Request (PAR)
•  Experience with DOORS for requirements development and traceability
•  Experience with CORE for system architecture modeling
•  Experience with system modeling languages, approaches, and methodologies, such as SysML
• Experience with Agile
