Sr. / Principal DevOps Engineer (Top Secret) – Space / Satellite Systems (El Segundo, CA, Dulles, VA, or Colorado Springs, CO)
R10087252
Days (United States of America)

We have an opening for a DevOps Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Tactical Space organization. This position will be in El Segundo, CA, Dulles, VA, or Colorado Springs, CO.  This work will primarily be performed in a SCIF environment so onsite work is required.

Job Responsibilities

In this role you will:

  • Automate cloud native technologies, deploy applications, and provision critical infrastructure
  • Work with microservices and distributed application architecture, such as containers, Kubernetes, and/or serverless technology
  • Work within the OpenStack cloud ecosystem to configure and deploy optimized infrastructure
  • Ensure monitoring and health of the resources deployed via Nagios or other enterprise management solutions
  • Document procedures used during deployment cycles
  • Experience with operational monitoring tools
  • Work with the fellow DevOps team and play a mentoring role to cross-train and grow technical talent across DevOps team and assist in troubleshooting tasks
  • Implement clean and maintainable infrastructure-as-code that adheres to industry best practices, terraform

To be successful in this role you should have:

  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment with quick turnaround times and shifting priorities
  • Proven troubleshooting skills and willingness to take on unfamiliar challenges 
  • 3+ years of experience with Infrastructure as Code (IaC), Terraform, & Ansible
  • 3+ years of experience in Linux tools/automation and shell scripting
  • 3+ years of experience in Python
  • 1+ years of experience with OpenStack management
  • 3+ years of experience with Docker containerization, Kubernetes (K8s) preferred
  • 3+ years of experience with continuous integration/deployment tools such as Jenkins or GitLab
  • 3+ years creating, executing, and troubleshooting complex, automated processes used to configure servers and/or deploy code

In this role, you will support High-Side DevOps team members and will be a technical lead for this team.

Basic Qualifications

  • Principal DevOps Engineer
    • Bachelor’s degree in a STEM field (ideally Computer Science or Engineering) and 5 years of experience; OR a master’s degree and 3 years: OR a PhD and 0 years. Experience can be considered in lieu of degree
    • 3+ year(s) of DevOps or software development experience
    • 3+ years in a technical leadership role managing technical objectives and Jira metrics reporting
    • U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top-Secret clearance.
  • Sr. Principal DevOps Engineer
    • Bachelor’s degree in a STEM field (ideally Computer Science or Engineering) and 9 years of experience; OR a master’s degree and 7 years: OR a PhD and 4 years. Experience can be considered in lieu of degree
    • 5+ year(s) of DevOps or software development experience
    • 5+ years in a technical leadership role managing technical objectives and Jira metrics reporting
    • U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top-Secret clearance.
