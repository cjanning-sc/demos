ACME Engineering Space Systems is looking for a Security Coordinator 3 to join our team of diverse and talented individuals at our Aurora, CO location.

 

The responsibilities include but are not limited to the following: Produces employee and guest badges, identification cards, and security reader cards. Processes security information and data for employment records, security clearances, citizenship verification and security facility accountability (i.e. keys, locks, VPN tokens). Collects and submits electronic fingerprints, handprints and optical reading into systems. Processes visit authorizations (incoming and outgoing) for employees and guests to gain access to restricted areas. Verifies accuracy of information prior to issuing clearances. Conduct secure message handling, shipping/receiving of classified material including courier support. Conduct briefings/INDOCs, and Out-processing/debriefs. Performs administrative duties as required.

 

**This role will be primarily focused on maintaining controlled security documents and information which includes document control and destruction including classified media. **

 

Basic Qualifications:

·       Active and current Top-Secret clearance with SCI eligibility OR current, active SCI access

·       High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years of additional education and/or experience related to security

        

Preferred Qualifications:

·       SIMS database experience

·       DISS experience

·       Lenel Experience
