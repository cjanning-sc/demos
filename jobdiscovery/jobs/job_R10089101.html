SCA Intelligence Computer-Based Training (CBT) Specialist
R10089101
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking an SCA Intelligence Computer-Based Training (CBT) Specialist to join our growing team of diverse professionals at Fort Carson in Colorado.

Responsibilities:

  • Serves as the lead operator for the Intelligence and Electronic Warfare Tactical Proficiency Trainer (IEWTPT) at the Fort Carson, Colorado Mission Training Complex (MTC) and its associated hub and spoke sites (Fort Hood, Riley, and Bliss)

  • Ensures IEWTPT capabilities are integrated into the planning and execution of collective intelligence training to include integrating intelligence systems (Technical Control Cell [TCC], Intelligence Low Overhead Driver, Warfighters Simulation [WARSIM] Intelligence Module, and Distributed Common Ground System-Army [DCGS-A]) to maximize training value

  • Provides expertise in the development, execution, and assessment of tailored and dynamic simulation stimulated training scenarios and preparatory events

  • Leverages a working knowledge of Army Mission Command Information Systems (MCIS), the collaborative estimate, and doctrinally-based, innovative decision support products they enable, and their integration within and among command posts

  • Provides introductory, pre-exercise, over the shoulder, and guided instruction to leaders and their Soldiers

SCA hourly rate: $33.80; H&W as specified in the applicable wage determination

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 10 years of experience as an All-Source Intelligence analyst or primary staff member at the Brigade, Division, or Corps level

    • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 8 years of experience as an All-Source Intelligence analyst or primary staff member at the Brigade, Division, or Corps level

    • Master’s degree with a minimum of 6 years of experience as an All-Source Intelligence analyst or primary staff member at the Brigade, Division, or Corps level

  • Prior experience as a military trainer with one of the following:

    • Brigade-level or higher S2/G2, Divisional, or higher level Analysis and Control Element (ACE) Chief

    • Division level all-source intelligence warrant officer

    • Senior Non-Commissioned Officer (NCO)

    • Division or Corps-level G2 operations officer

    • Combat Training Center (CTC) Observer/Controller

  • Must have a current U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Top Secret (TS) Security clearance with the ability to pass a Counterintelligence (CI) polygraph

  • Experience working in the Intelligence Warfighting Function to include all-source intelligence and information collection management operations, and a general understanding of connectivity and capabilities of automated Military Intelligence Systems

  • Must be capable of obtaining Secret Internet Protocol Router Network (SIPRNET) and Joint Worldwide Intelligence Communication System (JWICS) accounts within a 90-day period and must actively maintain these accounts

  • Must be capable of obtaining CompTIA certification in A+, Security+, or Network+ as per DoD 8570 requirements within 180-day period and must actively maintain this certification

  • Must be able to travel as needed (up to 50% of the time)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Possesses a National Security Agency Network (NSAnet) account

  • Possesses a current CI-scope polygraph (CSP) and has completed all mandatory oversight training

    • Training includes:  OVSC1000: NSA/CSS Intelligence Oversight Training, OVSC1100: Overview of Signals Intelligence Authorities and OVSC1800: USSID SP0018 Training

  • CompTIA certification in A+, Security+ or Network+ as per DoD 8570 requirements
