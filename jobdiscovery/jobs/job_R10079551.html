Embark on a career putting innovative, reliable, and agile products and ideas into orbit, and beyond. Our space systems connect and protect millions of people on earth every day, now and for the future. Explore your future and launch your career today.

ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal / Sr. Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Payloads team on the Sentinel (GBSD) program.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Apply the principles of engineering to design components and systems using applicable standards and specifications
  • Participate in technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for mechanical, electrical and integrated systems
  • Perform requirements analysis, develop source data for requirements, generate test requirements and prepare test reports
  • Verify specifications and designs through modeling and simulation, test data processing, and performance analysis
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Read electrical circuit diagrams/schematics and conduct electrical integration
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Develop Basis of Estimates (BOEs) for the systems engineering function
  • Interface with AF customer

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be filled at a Principal or Sr. Principal level

  • Minimum of a Bachelor of Science in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM)
  • A minimum 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with a PhD (Sr. Principal requires 9 years with a BS; 7 years with a MS; or 4 years with a PhD)
  • Experience with digital and analog communications protocols/ electrical circuit design
  • U.S. Citizenship and an Active Secret Security Clearance or higher awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Top Secret Security Clearance
  • Experience with nuclear weapon design
  • 5 years of experience with ICBM Weapon System
  • Experience with any of the following tools and languages: CORE, DOORS, Rhapsody, Cameo, ModelCenter, MagicDraw, Easy Power, Prima Vera, UML, SysML, Mentor Graphics, Mentor Capital
  • 5 years of experience in Systems Engineering in any of the following areas:
    • Requirements Management: MBSE, Functional Block Diagrams, Specification Trees
    • Systems and end item requirements validation and verification
    • Design: Systems Design, Systems Architecture, Systems Integration, N2 Analysis, Trade Studies
    • General knowledge of multiple engineering disciplines: Safety, Nuclear Hardness, Nuclear Surety, Material Science, Environmental, Parts and Logistics, etc.
    • Failure Modes, Effects, and Criticality Analysis
    • Project Management
    • Decision Making: Risk Management, Cost-Risk-Benefit Analysis
