The Mechanical Engineering group is hiring for a Sr. Principal Designer/Checker to join our Mechanical Engineering Design team within the Launch Vehicle Division at our Chandler, AZ facility.

Essential Functions:

  • Work within the ACME Engineering Design/Drafting group and will be responsible for checking drawing/design packages for proper content and to ensure compliance with applicable industry, military, and ASME 3D modeling standards
  • Check designs for fit, function, manufacturability, and economy of fabrication
  • Review of tolerance analysis, hardware selection and fitment on assembly prints
  • Review mechanical drawings with appropriate personnel verifying completed work, checking dimensions, materials and quantities to be used
  • Utilize expertise in GD&T to resolve interpretation issues on drawings 
  • Verify 3D models submitted to manufacturing utilizing Siemens NX managed with Teamcenter
  • Liaison with various engineering disciplines, manufacturing, quality, and vendors
  • Model fully defined components and assemblies efficiently using Siemens NX managed with Teamcenter and create/revise parametric drawings in conformance with ASME modeling and drawing standards
  • As requested, may supervise and direct the activities of staff
  • This position may be offered at Hybrid or Full Remote Work Schedule       

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma and 6 years directly related experience
  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret security clearance, or higher
  • Direct experience with Siemens NX, Teamcenter, Word, Excel, and Windows
  • Experience with geometric dimensioning and tolerating (GD&T) per ASME Y14.5-2009. 
  • Experience with weld design per AWS D17.1 and D1.1.  AWS CWI certification Preferred. 
  • Experience with Digital Product Definition Practices per ASME Y14.41
  • Knowledge of standard manufacturing processes including CNC Mill and Lathe operations
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment
  • Candidate must be comfortable working extended periods of time at a workstation
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work quickly, accurately, and exhibit a large degree of patience while under schedule pressure
  • Strong time management and organizational skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 2-year Technical Degree in Drafting or Engineering
  • Active DoD Secret Security clearance, or higher
  • Working knowledge of ASME Y14.100 and Mil-Standard 100
  • Experience working with DoD programs
  • GDTP Professional Certification Preferred
