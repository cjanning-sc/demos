ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Principal Software Engineer OR Senior Principal Software Engineer in Oklahoma City, OK.  The successful candidate will be assigned to develop and deliver software products for various classified programs.

This position may be filled at either the Principal Software Engineer OR Senior Principal Software Engineer level.

Basic Requirements for Principal Software Engineer:
• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years of Software engineering experience; OR a Master's Degree in a STEM field with 3 years of experience; OR a PhD with 0 years of experience.
• Basic C++ OR Python knowledge
• Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Clearance AND ability to obtain and maintain Program Access (PAR). Secret clearance and PAR must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.


Basic Requirements for Senior Principal Software Engineer:
• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 9 years of Software engineering experience; OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 7 years of Software engineering experience; OR a PhD in a STEM field with 4 years of experience.
• C++ OR Python knowledge
• Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Clearance AND ability to obtain and maintain Program Access (PAR). Secret clearance and PAR must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
