ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Senior Principal System Munition Lead for the Advanced Weapons Campaign to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our South Bay Aircraft Design Center organization. This role is located in Redondo Beach, California.

Essential Functions:

•             Demonstrate leadership principles – Integrity, respect, organizational, communication etc

•             Proficient in generating and maintaining models in Cameo EA; interface with subject matter experts, analysts, architects, designers, system engineers and specialty engineers to capture weapon structure and behavior

•             Participate in technical planning, verification and validation development, cost and risk considerations, and requirements analyses for integrated systems

•             Prepare and present content in internal and customer reviews, support CDRL deliveries

•             Develop unifying model techniques, procedures, and processes (for model development, tool integration, and team integration)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Basic qualifications for a Sr. Principal Engineer Systems include a Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 9 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 7 years of related engineering experience; OR a PhD in STEM with 4 years of related engineering experience.
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance with last investigation/adjudicated within the last 6 years
  • Model Based Systems Engineer (MBSE) experience:
  • Minimum 3 years Cameo EA experience (or similar tool)
  • Minimum 3 years of multi-disciplinary team leadership/management

Preferred Qualifications:

•             Experience with Company and DoD explosives/munitions handling, transportation, and storage logistics.

•             Familiarity with IADS software and IRIG-106 standards.

•             Experience with MIL-STD-882E and Non-Nuclear Munitions Safety Board (NNMSB).

•             Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal communication skills.

•             Experience in weapons development programs for the Army or Navy

•             Familiarity with environmental specifications for weapon systems (e.g. ATPD-2404, MIL-STD-810)
