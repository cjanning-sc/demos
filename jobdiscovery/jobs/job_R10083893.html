ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Production Test Technician 2 (2nd Shift) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Salt Lake City, Utah.

In this position you will:
• Perform test and evaluation of Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Systems.
• Troubleshoot and repair IMU Systems as required.
• Support production movement as needed to support daily requirements.
 
Basic Qualifications:
• Typical Minimum Education / Experience: High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience.
• Ability to read and understand Assembly Instruction and Test Procedures
• Experience in using Digital Multi-meter’s and other lab test equipment
• US Citizenship is required
 
Preferred Qualifications:
• Previous experience working in an Instrument Test facility
• Previous experience working closely with Test and Manufacturing engineers
• Previous experience trouble-shooting navigational Instruments
• Associates Degree
