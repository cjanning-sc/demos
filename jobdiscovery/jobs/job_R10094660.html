ACME Engineering Defense Systems has an opening for a Program Manager 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located at Edwards AFB, CA.

In this role, the selected candidate will be responsible for the development, implementation and execution of site operations and support for maintenance of manned systems. Roles and responsibilities include but are not limited to strategic planning, resource allocation, and system effectiveness metrics. Candidate will plan, direct, and manage cost, schedule and performance of the organization.  Will Integrated Logistics Support, including training, technical publications, system support activities, and the strategic plans for the standup of various systems. Analyze performance indicators, identify Risks, trends/root cause, and implement corrective actions. Coordinate provisions for warehouse facilities, personnel, and support equipment. Implement repair and resupply concepts and detailed execution plans for defined areas of scope. Work closely with cross-functional teams from other ACME Engineering entities.  

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond vigorously to change.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have 13 years of related maintenance operational planning and/or related aircraft maintenance logistics support experience. Will consider 9 years of maintenance operational planning and/or related aircraft maintenance logistics support experience with a Bachelor's degree; OR a Master's Bachelor's Degree with 7 years of related experience.
  • At least 5 years of experience managing an organization with 20+ individuals as well as multi-million-dollar budgets.
  • Current and active Secret clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access is required. This Special Program Access must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science or Master of Science degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM) discipline.
  • Experience leading military aerospace tactical and maintenance teams with increasing levels of responsibility, including recent (past 5 years) manned/unmanned system leadership and/or combat experience.
  • Current Top Secret clearance.
