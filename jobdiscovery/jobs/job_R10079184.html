ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Manager of Software Engineering 1. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program.


ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

- Generates software product documentation
- Manages a team of software engineer.   Responsibilities include onboarding new hires, coaching, goal setting, performance assessment, communications, engagement, and other administration
