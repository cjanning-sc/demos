ACME Engineering’s Mission Systems sector is looking for a Manager, Programs Level 3 to support the Navigation and Cockpit Systems business unit in Woodland Hills, CA.  This will be a Hybrid position requiring to be onsite twice a week.

The primary objective will be the delivery of all contractual requirements on cost and on schedule, while achieving all technical requirements and creating customer intimacy to support value creation strategies and actions.  The individual will lead a cross-functional organization aligned to common program performance goals.

 Program Managers are responsible for leading all phases of the program life cycle from inception (proposal development) through startup, execution, and completion (contract closeout).  Primary responsibilities are the management of cost, schedule, and technical performance of company programs or subsystems and include, but are not limited to:

  • Using analytical tool and data provide guidance and input to a cross-functional Integrated Program Team (IPT) to meet program cost, schedule, and technical performance objectives
  • Measuring and reporting program performance
  • Delivering presentations to customers, executive management, and other program stakeholders
  • Participating in the negotiation of contracts, contract changes, specifications, operating budgets, schedule milestones, and key terms and conditions
  • Creation, review and finalization of the program Statement of Work (SOW)
  • Identification, distribution, tracking, and completion of program requirements
  • Establishment and management of the program and subordinate baselines
  • Development and adherence to budget baselines utilizing Earned Value Management (EVM) or similar cost & schedule control methodologies and tools
  • Adherence to all internal processes, policies, and applicable industry standards
  • Ensuring program team understands and adheres to contract scope, and manages change through established processes
  • Development and adherence to master plans and schedules
  • Conducting thorough risk & opportunity management practices including identification, mitigation and realization

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in a technical discipline plus (15) years' experience leading teams in program execution. Experience in managing system design and development phase and performing SOW with responsibility for the planning, execution, cost control, scheduling, risk management, technical performance and profit & loss responsibility
  • Experience leading the performance of tasks on schedule, at cost and achieving all requirements as either project lead, integrated program team or cost account manager
  • Demonstrated success leading teams/and or organizations to achieve a common goal
  • Development of program plans and integrated master schedule
  • Knowledge of earned value management system (EVMS)
  • Experience with schedule management, program financial controls, business acquisition process, leading planning activities, and defining/tracking a wide range of program metrics.
  • Knowledge of Risk and Opportunity Management
  • Strong understanding and proven experience effectively working within and through a corporate culture
  • Expertise leading process improvement, organizational effectiveness, or operational planning.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in Business Administration or other relevant graduate degree - Five plus (5+) years in Program Management, Engineering, and Business Development leadership roles
  • Knowledge of and experience in NGMS’ business areas, including business models, structure and programs
  • Knowledge of major US DOD acquisition organizations, military customers, and/or military planning/requirements organizations
  • Capture experience that spans DoD, Commercial, and International captures
