ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Software Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position can be filled in Huntsville, AL and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will support and lead Software development in the Launch Guidance and Control sub-IPT. Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

• Derive and allocate requirements into detailed design

• Designs, develops and tests software product functionality

• Collaborates with Systems and Quality Engineers to ensure software requirements are met

• Performs software peer reviews, version control, automated tests, software analysis, continuous integration, and continuous delivery in accordance with DevSecOps processes and best practices

• Generates software product documentation

Job Qualifications:

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

• A Bachelor and 5 years of experience; 3 years with Master’s Degree; 0 years with PhD or 4 additional years in lieu of a degree

• Minimum 2 years experience in software development in at least one object-oriented programming language such as Java or C++

• Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering languages (SysML/UML) and concepts

• Experience with UI design and development, or experience Interpreting complex algorithms and requirements for implementation

• Experience as technical lead on a software engineering team or project

• Experience presenting to program management, other technical managers and customers

• Active DoD Secret clearance, granted or re-investigated within last 6 years
