ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an A/C Mechanical Environmental Control System (ECS) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in El Segundo, CA.

In this role, the A/C Mechanical Environmental Control System (ECS) will be expected to:

  • Layout, assembly, installation, and rework of aircraft parts, assemblies including necessary incidental fabrication of pre-production, development or production aircraft.

  • Requires the individual to determine methods and sequence of Environmental Control System (ECS) assembly, installation, repair, rework, and modification operations on pre-production aircraft. Requires the individual to determine methods and sequence of electrical and electronic assembly, installation, repair, rework and modification operations on pre-production, development or production aircraft.

  • Lays out, locates, assemble, install, check out, and rework intricate ECS lines.

  • Complete any or all ECS plumbing rework, squawks, EO’s and part shortages, make or conduct high pressure checks of completely installed ECS immediately prior to operational checkout.

  • This includes operating test stand, applying pressure, eliminating leaks, and making necessary repairs and adjustments.

  • Works from all types of complex blueprints, E.O.’s and production illustrations; is often required to work without adequate tooling or on jobs where engineering and tooling information is incomplete.

  • May be required to work in other fields incidental to, and in connection with, completion of installation of a major plumbing system.

  • Install, hook up, and adjust such items as turbines, heat exchangers, valves, cylinders, struts, pumps, instruments, and lines.

  • May work with the assistance of lower classified employees; may assist higher classified workers on occasion.

  • Direct and train other employees as assigned.

  • Perform work of the level described herein and interpret complex blueprints and use arithmetic involving decimals and fractions.

  • May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

  • Must be able to utilize computer-based systems to perform duties.

  • May be required to work in confined space.

  • Maintain required certifications and qualifications on area of specialization.

  • Must be willing/able to work any shift or schedule.

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret or Top-Secret Clearance as well as Program Special access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent.

  • Must have at least 3 1/2 years of related Mechanical Experience
