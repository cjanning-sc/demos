ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Sr. Principal Structural Dynamics Engineer in support of an advanced development program on our Vehicle Engineering team.  This position will be in Melbourne, FL and relocation assistance is available.

Job Summary:

Acts as a subject matter expert specializing in vehicle structural dynamics supporting an advanced development program. Will serve as technical lead in all matters related to vibration, vibro-acoustics, shock response and environment generation.

The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in structural dynamic analysis with concentration in flow-structure coupling, aeroacoustics analysis, and random vibration analysis. In addition, the successful candidate will be a highly motivated self-starter and will be proactive, forward-thinking, and able to lead a small team to rapid resolution of complex problems.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree and 9+ years of related experience, OR Master’s degree and 7+ years of related experience, OR a Ph.D. and 4+ years of related experience

  • At least one degree must be in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline from an accredited university

  • Aircraft dynamics experience

  • Active DoD Secret clearance or higher with background investigation completed within the last 6 years or under continuous evaluation

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain Program Access prior to the commencement of employment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in Engineering with an emphasis in aircraft loads and dynamics (flight loads, ground loads, flutter and aeroelasticity, etc.)

  • Strong foundation in structural dynamic analysis with concentration in flow-structure coupling, aeroacoustics analysis, and random vibration analysis

  • Experience with vibration, vibro-acoustics, shock response and environment generation

  • Experience in the use of industry standard loads and dynamics analysis software: NASTRAN, PATRAN, FEMAP, ZONAIR, ZAERO

  • Experience with MATLAB and CAD Tools (NX)

  • Prior experience leading a project or team
