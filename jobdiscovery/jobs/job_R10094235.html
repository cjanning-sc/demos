R10094235
Days (United States of America)

ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Cyber Info Assurance Analyst - Information System Security Officer (ISSO) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Schriever Space Force Base, Colorado Springs, CO

Position Overview:

  • Primarily responsible for maintaining documentation, account creation process and performing vulnerability scans for C2BMC located at Schriever SFB, Colorado.
  • Provide feedback to the Cyber Operations Lead and work closely with other C2BMC ISSOs.
  • Participate in the coordination of system security and connection authorizations to C2BMC systems.
  • Provide input to POA&M and memorandum of understanding/agreements. Assist in providing status on site security posture, such as, IAVA compliance and CTO status.
  • Candidate must be a self-starting problem solver, capable of planning, coordinating, directing and controlling their individual projects, and possess the capability to work with limited supervision.
  • Performs other related duties and assignments as required.
     

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • Knowledge of modern computer systems, client/server, LAN/WAN and Network concepts, modern network management and monitoring concepts
  • Proficient in Microsoft Windows and Red Hat Linux (RHEL) operating systems
  • Hands-on experience with vulnerability scanners
  • Working knowledge and proficiency in technical writing

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of IA concepts such as patch management, multi-factor authentication, host-based security, intrusion detection and defense-in-depth is preferred.
  • Previous experience and sound knowledge of C2BMC System or Missile Defense
