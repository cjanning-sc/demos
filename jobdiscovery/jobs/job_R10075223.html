ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do:

Apply their experience and knowledge in technical design, build and verification of complex systems to the full integration of specialty engineering responsibilities into the GBSD system

Collaborate with both internal and external teams to integrate specialty engineering domains across all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance, and disposal

Maintain technical cognizance of subsystem interdependencies in support of maturing Specialty Systems Engineering analyses/products and identifying and resolving gaps

Engage in formal and informal mentoring to support knowledge transfer and talent development

Implement/Support MBSE strategy for the Architecture

Implement/Support Requirements Verification strategy for the architecture

Align specialty engineering to the GBSD technical planning, system integration, requirements verification, modeling and validation efforts

  • A minimum 9 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree in STEM; 7 years with a Master’s; or 4 years with PhD
