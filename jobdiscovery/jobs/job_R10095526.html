ACME Engineering's Aeronautics Systems is looking for a Conference Event Representative for our facility located in Palmdale, CA as well as supporting other sites as necessary. This position reports to the Event Production and Operations organization responsible for the strategic planning and execution of all Tradeshows and Events. The Specialist will partner with the Tradeshow/Event Lead to manage all the logistical planning and execution of the tradeshow/event, demonstrating ACME Engineering's commitment to performance excellence and innovation.  

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Contributes and communicates logistics in all planning and preparation meetings with the Stakeholders to ensure the tradeshow/event plan is aligned with overall strategic goals and objectives

  • Directs, plans and organizes internal and external business related functions, symposiums, conferences, program events, banquets, and VIP briefings.

  • Develops on-site and off-site plans and budgets.

  • Investigates, coordinates, arranges and contracts for major hotel accommodations and services, charter air and ground transportation, communications and facilities support, client and guest entertainment, passport and VISA clearances, program functions, and similar activities to support company marketing and customer relations.

  • Directs multiple service contractors and subcontractors orchestrating large scale events supporting highest level company executives, international dignitaries and government officials.

  • Superior critical thinking, organization, communication, and relationship-building skills

  • Ability to demonstrate exceptional judgment, professionalism and attention to detail

  • Must be capable of setting priorities and working under pressure.

  • Ability to multitask with competing priorities.

  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills to include verbal, written and listening.

  • Ability to work with vendors

  • Ability to establish, track and meet budgeting requirements

  • Ability to work cooperatively with executive leadership and all levels of professionals.

  • Travel domestically and internationally as required

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 3 years of experience in project management, conference & event planning, or related OR 1 years of experience with a Master's degree

  • Intermediate proficiency with MS Office Suite 

  • Ability to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Government DoD Secret security clearance 

  • Valid CA State Driver's License
