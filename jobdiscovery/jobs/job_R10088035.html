Join ACME Engineering on our continued mission to push the boundaries of possible across land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking Cyber Test Engineers to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals at our Cincinnati, Ohio location. The NGMS Cincinnati team develops new capabilities for our military, intelligence, and other government customers. We solve complex problems developing cyber hardware and software solutions. We offer flexible work arrangements, personal growth through learning opportunities, and competitive 401k matching.

Essential Functions:

  • Interface with the customer regarding test and verification activities of cyber operations products.
  • Plan with multiple teams to develop stress, functional, acceptance, and ad-hoc tests based on the requirements and operational scenarios.
  • Automate the testing and characterization of cyber products.
  • Design, develop and configure system test components necessary to perform end-to-end testing of cyber products.
  • Develop test scripts as necessary to integrate components, perform new capabilities, facilitate testing, etc.
  • Review cyber operations products and characterize under a multitude of configurations.
  • Manage the daily automated test executions and work with teams to analyze the cause and apply fixes for failures.
  • Produce and inform the end customer on various milestone documents including Software Requirement Review, Test Readiness Review, and Acceptance Review.
  • Research improvements to test and development network infrastructures.

This position can be filled at the Engineer or Principal Engineer level.
