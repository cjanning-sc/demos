ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Systems Under Test, Manager of Test Engineering 2, to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

This position will be located in Palmdale or Edwards, AFB, CA.

  • The selected candidate will provide technical products and planning coordination for the Test IPT as the manager for the system under test.

  • Will fill in as needed as the Cost Account Manager overseeing a substantial operating budget producing budget forecasting and analysis.

  • Position will be the lead point of contact for the test team and will coordinate and oversee all maintenance and test activities for the system under test.

  • Preferred Qualifications:

  • Excellent communications skills to include ability to author technical reports and correspondence

  • Experience briefing managers, senior program leadership and customers.
