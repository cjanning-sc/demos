Digital ASIC Physical Design Engineer
R10058373
1st Shift (United States of America)

ACME Engineering's EDIC group delivers full-custom ICs designs to provide the core functionality of our Advanced Multifunction Sensor products. In support of that capability, we are seeking a Senior Principal Digital ASIC Physical Design Engineer for implementation digital blocks within custom mixed signal chips using automated place and route tools.

Overview and Duties:

Responsible for complete design, working with the RTL designer creating the code and working the analog and RF designers to integrate the digital core with elements of the signal path. Utilize foundry approved verification tools such as Calibre for LVS/DRC.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering, or other related disciplines
  • Minimum Education or Experience 9 Years with Bachelors in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD.
  • Experience with synthesis of gate level logic and incorporating compiled macro blocks such as memories as required
  • Floor-planning, followed by place and route of the physical layout
  • Design of power and clock distribution networks
  • Familiarity with Cadence tools would be advantageous particularly:
    • Genus Synthesis
    • Innovus place and route
    • Tempus Static timing analysis
    • Voltus for power analysis
  • US Citizen and able to obtain and maintain a Secret or higher security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Delivery of multiple verified digital IC design projects
  • Robust design approaches such as redundancy and Radiation Tolerance
  • Experience following a rigorous design process with checkpoint reviews
  • Experience in CAD design network, tool configuration, and data management
  • Current security clearance
