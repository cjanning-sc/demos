ACME Engineering in Azusa, CA is seeking a level Principal (Level 3) or Sr. Principal (Level 4) Environmental Test Engineer experienced in payload level environmental testing of space products including thermal vacuum, thermal cycle and vibration.  The selected candidate will provide oversight to environmental laboratory resource planning, maintenance and calibration of facility equipment, test execution and process improvements, and personnel training.   

This position will report on-site daily to our Azusa campus. We can accommodate partial telework but this is fully dependent upon business/team/program need so not always going to be a specific schedule.

Job Duties:

  • Oversee and direct engineering technicians and related activities
  • Responsibility for efficiency and organization of vibration facilities and associated hardware / software equipment. 
  • Author test procedures in coordination with program requirements.  Conduct and oversee execution of testing procedures on customer test articles per work instructions and training procedures. 
  • Complete technical reports, test results, and all required documentation
  • Complete test readiness reviews (TRR’S)/CTP’s/CTB’s
  • Coordinate testing programs and test equipment use in cooperation with program management, test resources and personnel.
  • Coordinate work shifts and extended coverage to support program schedules as required.
  • Oversee a regular maintenance schedule for all vibration test systems and associated equipment 
  • Reviewing of basic mechanical and electrical drawings.

Basic Qualifications: (Principal Level 3) 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering Mathematics) field & 5 years of related test engineering experience, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 3 years of related test engineering experience
  • Experience working in a testing lab environment and maintaining test lab equipment
  • Experience operating electromagnetic vibration systems and thermal chambers
  • Experience in Space industry standards tests (EMI/EMC, Qualification Tests, Vibration, Shock, etc.)
  • Experience in conducting Test Readiness Reviews
  • Experience with highly monitored schedule driven programs and proven records of on time goal accomplishments
  • During testing, the ability to work extended hours and off shifts 
  • Demonstrated experiences of good mechanical abilities and background. 
  • Ability to obtain & maintain a DoD Secret level clearance (US Citizenship Required)

Basic Qualifications: (Sr. Principal Level 4)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering Mathematics) field & 9 years of related test engineering experience, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 7 years of related test engineering experience
  • Experience working in a testing lab environment and maintaining test lab equipment
  • Experience operating electromagnetic vibration systems and thermal chambers
  • Experience in Space industry standards tests (EMI/EMC, Qualification Tests, Vibration, Shock, etc.)
  • Experience in conducting Test Readiness Reviews
  • Experience with highly monitored schedule driven programs and proven records of on time goal accomplishments
  • During testing, the ability to work extended hours and off shifts 
  • Demonstrated experiences of good mechanical abilities and background. 
  • Ability to obtain & maintain a DoD Secret level clearance (US Citizenship Required)


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s Degree in a STEM field
  • 6+ years of test engineering experience
  • Current & active security clearance
  • Demonstrated experience managing, training, and motivating work teams
  • Experience setting up and troubleshooting environmental test setups including fixture development, flight hardware installation, instrumentation and HW/SW interfaces. 
  • Familiarity with Unholtz-Dickie and ETS electro dynamic shakers; Vibration Research and M+P Controllers; data recorders, high speed DAQ’s; oscilloscopes; PCB signal conditioners, and accelerometers
  • Experience with response limiting vibration testing and evaluating power spectral density (PSD) data real-time
  • Experience with Spectral Dynamics controllers (Linux OS) and Piranha High Speed Data Acquisition Systems
  • Experience setting up and troubleshooting vibration test setups including fixture evaluations, flight hardware installation to vibration fixtures, instrumenting fixtures and flight hardware with accelerometers. 
  • Experience developing and evaluating shock and vibration models; finite element model development experience
  • Comfortable extracting models and understanding designs made in CAD packages
  • MATLAB/Labview knowledge is a plus.
