Principal Industrial Engineer

Join ACME Engineering on our continued mission to push the boundaries of possible across land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. Enjoy a culture where your voice is valued and start contributing to our team of passionate professionals providing real-life solutions to our world’s biggest challenges. We take pride in creating purposeful work and allowing our employees to grow and achieve their goals every day by Defining Possible. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is searching for a Level 2 Industrial Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. Our team supports operations and business improvement across 1800 employees located in Utah, Ohio, California and Mississippi. This position will be located in Clearfield, UT.

Travel is estimated to be less than 10%.

Responsibilities:

  • Participate in continuous improvement events to develop, define and improve processes in the forming and finishing of aircraft composite structures
  • Analyze various manufacturing processes for opportunities to minimize scrap, reduce cost, increase capacity and optimize schedule.
  • Provide support to the manufacturing floor by working proactively with cross-functional teams to understand frontline problems and implement cost-saving, impactful solutions.
  • Generate metrics and conducts studies pertaining to efficiency, cycle times, work-in-progress, manpower, cost performance, and product delivery.
  • Partner with manufacturing management to develop and track recovery plans that instill confidence with program management and customers, even through unforeseen challenges.
  • Apply systems thinking in the development of metrics to improve product flow from the release of raw materials through product delivery.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters
  • Experience with Continuous Improvement methodologies including, Toyota Production System, Theory of Constraints, Six Sigma and Agile manufacturing
  • Ability to work on a manufacturing floor 
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance
  • Strong expertise using the full suite of Microsoft Office Tools

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current DoD clearance
  • Special Program Access
  • Experience working with cross-functional teams in a manufacturing or business environment
