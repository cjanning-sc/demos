ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Electrical Engineer / Principal Electrical Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will be responsible for cable designs, drawings development, cable routing/layout for multiple weapon system facilities, conduit design/routing, cable part selection, installation drawings support, data packages creation and support test plan development.  Development includes both power and signal cables.  You will be doing all this as part of the Launch Equipment Distribution System Team.

Roles & Responsibilities:

• Developing innovative solutions and elaborating on design concepts

• Perform detailed cable design including part selection

• Determining the technical principles involved, identifying the design path forward and engineering characteristics to ensure consistency with the specifications and requirements

• Releasing results as “technical documents” after review by the senior members of the team.

• Evaluating completed designs with supervisor and other technical groups and making modifications necessary to achieve the most ideal design.

• Developing and coordinating test plans for Integration and Test activities

• Working in a cross functional environment (electrical engineering, software engineering, systems engineering, test engineering, production engineering, etc.) to translate customer requirements

• Generate documentation and brief effectively and clearly to multiple program boards and leadership

•Verify specifications and designs through modeling and simulation, test data processing, and performance analysis

• Support the fabrication team for hardware builds

• Provide supplier technical oversight.

• Interface with AF Customer
