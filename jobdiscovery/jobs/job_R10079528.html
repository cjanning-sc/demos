Job Description:

The qualified candidate will perform detailed design of special test equipment and develop testing methods to facilitate electrical tests, system integration, qualification and acceptance test of space electronic products.   Design responsibilities include electronic circuit design, mechanical design and development of LabView test software.  Responsibilities will also include developing test plans and procedures to assure product conforms to customer requirements; Conducting product qualification, environmental and acceptance level testing; Compiling test data and generation of test reports.

Basic Qualifications:

Principal Engineer:

  • BS in Electrical Engineering or STEM degree with a minimum of 5 years of experience with the development test plans and procedures and design experience; or MS degree with 3 years of exp; or PhD, or 9 years of exp in lieu of degree.

  • Solid multi-disciplined electronics background, in power systems, digital and analog electronics and electronic systems, experience designing electronic circuits and LabView Test Software.

  • Familiarity with bench test equipment (oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, arbitrary waveform generators, etc.), automated software driven test equipment (LabView) and custom board, box and system level test set design.

Sr Principal Engineer:

  • BS in Electrical Engineering or STEM degree with a minimum of 9 years of experience with the development test plans and procedures and design experience; or MS degree with 7 years of exp; or PhD with 4 years of exp; or 13 years of exp in lieu of degree.

  • Solid multi-disciplined electronics background, in power systems, digital and analog electronics and electronic systems, experience designing electronic circuits and LabView Test Software.

  • Familiarity with bench test equipment (oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, arbitrary waveform generators, etc.), automated software driven test equipment (LabView) and custom board, box and system level test set design.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • BSEE in Electrical

  • Experience with spaceflight electronics

  • Experience in the Aerospace industry
