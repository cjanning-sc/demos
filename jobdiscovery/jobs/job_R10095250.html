We are looking for you to join our team as an Associate Embedded Software Engineer / Embedded Software engineer based out of Rolling Meadows, IL.

As an Associate Embedded Software Engineer/Embedded Software Engineer at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our mission to deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment and shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

ACME Engineering is looking to add to our talented team of Embedded Software Engineers. Our engineers are self-starters who are part of a team of software professionals developing world-class software to meet our customer’s high expectations. The Embedded Software Engineer designs, develops, operates, and maintains software and firmware components and computing systems software to be applied to and integrated with engineering, scientific and manufacturing requirements. The individual shall apply the appropriate standards, processes, procedures, and tools throughout the system development life cycle to support the generation of engineering applications and products within the Business Unit. This includes interfacing with customers, suppliers, application users and other technical and support personnel. The ideal individual will have experience including the full software development life cycle, which includes requirements, design, coding, testing, and integration.

This position can be filled at an Associate Embedded Software Engineer or Embedded Software Engineer level:

Basic Qualifications for Associate Embedded Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or applicable engineering industry experience or in lieu of degree an additional 4 years of experience is required.
  • Familiar with Object Oriented Programming in C++
  • Familiarity with Software architecture (design patterns, objected oriented design)
  • Familiarity with scripting languages (Python, Bash, Batch, etc)
  • Familiarity with operating system concepts for embedded systems
  • Familiarity with software development methodologies and frameworks such as Waterfall and/or Agile Software Development Lifecycle
  • Familiarity with various version control systems (Git, Gitlabs, Github, Bitbucket, ClearCase. etc) and Agile software planning tools (Rally, Jira, etc)
  • Must have interim Secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain Secret security clearance.
