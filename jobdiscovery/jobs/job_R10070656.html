Principal Technical Services Project Management

1st Shift (United States of America)

The Principal Technical Services Project Management (OPM) position supports production, business, and other processes in a team based manufacturing environment. Performs activities in a variety of cross functional areas including production planning and control, total quality management, systems, manufacturing, equipment and facilities engineering, material management, and process reengineering. The incumbent may facilitate self-directed work teams, provide training in core skill areas to teams and peers, and interact with internal and external customers with respect to product planning, problem resolution, and process improvement. Incumbents are generally experienced in one or more areas of manufacturing operations.

Specific Responsibilities will include:

- The Principal Technical Services Project Management will be the primary Manufacturing interface with the Program Office, engineering, Supply Chain Management and Mission Assurance on all aspects of assigned programs.

- Responsible for scheduling and execution of programs, while monitoring and addressing all cost, schedule and quality issues.

- Lead multifunctional teams, including Operations functional support organizations, to ensure all program and manufacturing goals and objectives are met.

- Represent the organization as a prime contact on contracts or programs. Interact with internal and external personnel on significant matters often requiring coordination between organizations.

- Oversee the generation of proposal inputs, including Basis of Estimate, Resource code mix, estimate rationale, bid codes, overhead, labor, manufacturing and personnel capacity, and risk / opportunity tracking.

- Responsible for the overall performance of the Operations Team's activities to assure compliance with company objectives and obligations.

- Monthly reconciliation of Sales Order WBS to MRP WBS for accuracy and consistency.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Project Management:

Bachelor's degree and 6 years of relevant work experience

- Demonstrated experience working on teams and projects in a leadership capacity

- Demonstrated proficiency in the Microsoft Office Excel (including pivot tables, V-Look up's)

- This position requires a U.S. Citizenship and ability to obtain a security clearance

- High desire to learn with a meticulous attention to detail.

Basic Qualifications for Sr Principal Project Management:

Bachelor's degree and 10 years of relevant work experience

- Demonstrated experience working on teams and projects in a leadership capacity

- Demonstrated proficiency in the Microsoft Office Excel (including pivot tables, V-Look up's)

- This position requires a U.S. Citizenship and ability to obtain a security clearance

- High desire to learn with a meticulous attention to detail.

Preferred Qualifications:

- General knowledge of manufacturing.

- Knowledge of Manufacturing Resource Planning (MRP), production planning, and material management.

- Ability to communicate, both written and verbal, with several layers of coworkers and management.

- Understanding of Earned Value reporting and analysis.

- Experience with proposal generation.
