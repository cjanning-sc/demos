ACME Engineering Technology Services is seeking a Sr. Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist to join its team of qualified, diverse professionals engaged in supporting dynamic, complex, and challenging programs.

Shall be responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases for specialized goods and/or services. Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, planning, operations, and program management. Owns contractual management of multiple suppliers, develops subcontract specifications, develop negotiation plans, assist, and review subcontract statements of work, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services. Execute teaming and non-disclosure agreements as needed.

Prepares proposal packages, analyzes, and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects, or recommends subcontractors, writes subcontracts, and awards finalized subcontracts. In conjunction with quality, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules. Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.

Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations. Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities. Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.  Utilizes process to drive quality, efficiency, continuous improvement to ensure that risk and mitigation strategies are identified and developed.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors' degree and a minimum 10 years of supply chain, contracts, business management and/or related field OR Master's degree and a minimum of 8 years of supply chain, contracts, business management and/or related field. 14 additional years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Ability to collaborate with program management and other multi-functional teams
  • Ability to multi-task and make sound decisions in a timely manner
  • Demonstrated strong interpersonal skills
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a very fast paced environment
  • Experience with Microsoft Office
  • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated strong leadership skills as well as the ability to multi-task in a challenging environment
  • Professional experience in a regulated industry; to include Aerospace & Defense, Insurance, Financial Services, Telecommunications
  • SAP experience
  • Working knowledge of FAR/DFARS and associated Federal Contracting regulations
  • Demonstrated experience with multiple contracting formats, including Firm Fixed Price and/or Cost Reimbursable,
  • Familiarity with terms and conditions, and/or special contract clauses/types such as: long term agreements and intellectual property agreements.
  • Demonstrated negotiation experience
