ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector is seeking a Principal Business Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be in Dulles, VA. The qualified applicant will be responsible for supporting the Tactical Space System Division’s Strategy and Business Development Operations.

Responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Creating and Managing the Division’s Tableau Dashboard portfolio; integrating data from multiple sources and functional teams to provide quantitative insights that guide executive decision making and business outcomes.
  • Managing the development, tracking, reporting, and forecasting of Business Development Operational Goals and Activities
  • Serve as the Division’s Salesforce Administrator, managing division user accounts, leading virtual training, and supporting data requests.
  • Managing the drafting and development of executive caliber deliverables, including but not limited to Board of Directors Reports; Monthly Management Reports; BD Operational Goals Reports; Sector Quarterly Reviews; and Pipeline Assessment Reports.
  • The candidate will manage and coordinate executive-level meetings and pipeline/opportunity reviews, to include coordination with Tactical Space Systems Division, and Sector BD Staff.
  • Selected candidate will interact with senior internal personnel on significant matters often requiring coordination between organizations
  • Other responsibilities may include supporting the development and execution of communication strategies and supporting long range strategic initiatives and campaigns as needed.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Finance, Information Systems, or related discipline (6 Years of experience with Bachelors; 4 Years of experience with Masters)
  • Four (4) years analytical experience.
  • Skilled with dashboarding software (e.g., Tableau, Tableau CRM, and/or Excel/Power Pivot/Power BI)
  • Experience with KPI / performance metric reporting and analysis
  • Familiarity with data visualization and data modeling tools and techniques.
  • Proven ability to create, compose and edit written materials, and to consolidate data from multiple sources into concise executive caliber summaries.
  • Superior analytical skills - ability to draw conclusions and actionable recommendations.
  • A result driven attitude and a proven capability of working in a collaborative environment across distributed locations and organizations.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Full knowledge of industry and NGC practices and processes.
  • Knowledge and experience in business plan development, market research and competitive analysis.
  • Prior Salesforce Administrator experience preferred.
  • Current TS/SCI & SAP
