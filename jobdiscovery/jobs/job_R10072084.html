ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Systems Integration and Test Engineer/Principal Systems Integration and Test Engineer based out of Rolling Meadows, IL. As a Systems Integration and Test Engineer/Principal Systems Integration and Test Engineer at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation.

What You’ll get to Do:

ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking Systems Engineering, Integration and Test (SEIT) engineers at our Rolling Meadows / Arlington Heights, IL location.  The selected candidates will report to the SEIT Department in the Infrared Countermeasures (IRCM) Department.

In Integration & Test, you will conduct integration testing and evaluate complex electronics systems from hardware/software design through aircraft installation.  Responsibilities for an Integration & Test Engineer may include generation of test plans, test procedures, test automation, test reports, and execution of integration test activities in System Integration Labs (SILs) or offsite locations. As a part of the Systems Engineering Integration & Test (SEIT) team, an Integration & Test engineer gains an understanding of the Systems Engineering process across a product's lifecycle and gains a full appreciation for how the systems operate. Your key responsibilities will be to ensure that the systems we make work – and work well.   You’ll be able to apply your knowledge and experience in EOIR systems, optics, detection and tracking.

This position can be filled at the Systems or Principal Systems level:

Basic Qualifications for Systems Integration and Test Engineer:

  • BS degree in Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, or related technical degree with 2+ years of related experience or MS degree in Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, or related technical field
  • Demonstrated proficiency in analytical thinking, problem solving skills and Systems Thinking mindset
  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills
  • Strong work ethic and the ability to work in an integrated product team (IPT) environment
  • Must have ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date. Once started, must have the ability to obtain full secret clearance and SAP clearance.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Systems Integration and Test Engineer:

  • BS degree in Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, or related technical degree with 5+ years of related experience or MS degree in Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, or related technical field with 3+ years related experience
  • Demonstrated proficiency in analytical thinking, problem solving skills and Systems Thinking mindset
  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills
  • Strong work ethic and the ability to work in an integrated product team (IPT) environment
  • Ability and willingness to work independently with little oversight or direction
  • Advanced troubleshooting of hardware, software and networking issues
  • Must have ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date. Once started, must have the ability to obtain full secret clearance and SAP clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of one of more of the following specialty areas for integration and test related to EOIR systems, optics, detection and tracking
  • Experience in an integration and test environment for complex electronic systems evaluation
  • Experience performing system verification and validation
  • Recent experience creating integration and test, design verification test and flight test plans and procedures
  • Electronic Warfare experience
  • Experience in development and earned value management of cost accounts and schedules as related to Systems Engineering tasks
  • Knowledge of MATLAB, C, C++, Java, Python, or other scripting languages
  • Working knowledge of DOORS and ClearCase/ClearQuest
  • Agile development experience a plus
  • Ability and willingness to travel 25%
  • Active Secret or Top Secret clearance and the ability to obtain a SAP (Special Access Program) clearance
