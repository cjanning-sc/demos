ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Project Analyst based out of Woodland Hills, CA. As a Project Analyst at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter.  Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation.  The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You’ll get to Do:

  • Handle Technical Problem Summaries (TPSs) in close coordination with the applicable Systems Engineering Integration & Test (SEIT) Lead and program team to ensure technical / non-technical process issues affecting various product lines are handled in an effective and timely manner
  • In support of the SEIT Lead, assign actions to Engineering organizations in both Woodland Hills, CA and Salt Lake City, UT and authorize budget and provide work orders to resolve technical and process problems
  • Process and coordinate the initial release of new factory SIL / test equipment (hardware & software), tooling and test procedures and do the same for all ongoing changes to these documents
  • Process and coordinate the release of new Prime Hardware (Product) drawings packages (including Prime Software) and do the same for all ongoing changes to these documents.
  • Identify the different types of drawings (i.e. detailed drawings, assembly drawings, arrangement drawings)
  • Work with affected engineering groups to provide guidance on drawing / family tree development
  • Multi-task through an occasional high stress environment to support engineering releases to achieve sales / program goals
  • Work daily with complex engineering documentation management systems such as Matrix/PDM (Enovia) and ISE
  • Gain an understanding of the business and customer priorities and expedite processing & coordination where needed
  • Ensure compliance to applicable Command Media

Basic Qualifications for a Project Analyst are:

  • Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience, a Master’s degree and 1 year experience, or 7 years relevant experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience with the processing of Engineering Changes (i.e. Technical Problem Summaries (TPSs) and Revision Notices (RNs)).
  • Experience with product lifecycle management software (i.e. Teamcenter).
  • Solid planning and organizational skills, as well as attention to detail.
  • Strong verbal communication and customer service skills in a teamwork environment.
  • US Citizenship is required in order to obtain and maintain security clearance
  • Ability to be onsite 80%

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Project Analyst are:

  • Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 6 years of relevant experience, a Master’s degree and 4 year experience, or 10 years relevant experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience with the processing of Engineering Changes (i.e. Technical Problem Summaries (TPSs) and Revision Notices (RNs)).
  • Experience with product lifecycle management software (i.e. Teamcenter).
  • Solid planning and organizational skills, as well as attention to detail.
  • Strong verbal communication and customer service skills in a teamwork environment.
  • US Citizenship is required in order to obtain and maintain security clearance
  • Ability to be onsite 80%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Cisco Matrix and Identity Services Engine (ISE).
  • Experience with Siemens Teamcenter.
  • Experience with Configuration Management principles.
  • General knowledge of electrical and mechanical systems and manufacturing processes.
  • Knowledge of SharePoint and 1ERP.
  • Possess a rudimentary understanding of the indenturing structure (i.e. detailed drawings lead to assembly drawings, which can lead to module drawings).
  • Possess a basic understanding of drawing re-identification criteria such as dash number “rolls” vs. going to a new basic drawing number for a mandatory non-re-workable change.
