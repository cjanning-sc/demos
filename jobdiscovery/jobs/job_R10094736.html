ACME Engineering Space Systems designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

Job Summary and Responsibilities

ACME Engineering Innovation Systems is currently seeking an experienced Systems Engineer to join the Minotaur Systems Engineering Team in our Chandler, AZ office.

The Systems Engineer will support numerous system engineering tasks.

  • Decompose, allocate, flow-down, and manage system, mission, and subsystem requirements.
  • Perform requirements verification/validation and produce requirements compliance verification documents.
  • Coordinate system test data reviews that will result in verification that 100% of telemetry parameters are functioning properly prior to launch.
  • Create original and/or revise existing specifications/interface definition documents, present at change control board meetings, and expedite documents through the signature approval process.
  • Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution, and mission operations.
  • Generates presentation materials for and attends customer and internal reviews.
  • Desirable to have strong schedule/project experience.
