ACME Engineering is currently seeking an Engineering Technician I to provide support to our Test and Research Area team at our Magna, UT facility.

Successful candidate will be required to review and implement test plans, drawings, specifications, and planning in support of various development projects and production. Skills needed include strong working knowledge and experience in majority of the following areas: sample fabrication and installation, operation of specialized operating equipment and tooling, handling operations, and safe use of various chemicals and bonding compounds. Ordering supplies as needed from vendors or other sources. Implementation of continuous improvement using 5S and Lean Manufacturing principles will be required. Job function requires use of standard computer skills including Microsoft Office, CAD and other software programs. Knowledge and skill in using hand tools, lathes, drills, saws, and other tools. Excellent customer service and organization skills are a must. Other job duties as Management directs.

Preferred Requirements:

•  Experience in setting up and running computerized CNC equipment or willing to learn. Preference will be given to those who have CNC experience.
•  Experience in set-up, fabrication, using a lathe, mill, and other carpentry equipment and able to trouble shoot equipment problems.
•  Ability to receive and follow specific instructions from procedures, prints, planning and supervision.
•  Good communication skills with ability to understand and communicate technical information with clarity.
•  Ability to read, interpret and implement technical drawings, specifications, and procedures.
•  Computer skills in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and database applications.
•  Experience with CAD or other Drafting software.
•  Ability to work without direct supervision.

Education Requirements:

•  High school diploma required.
•  Associate degree in technology/instrumentation/electronics preferred.
•  Will consider years of experience with certifications. 
•  Strong technical background in electronics and mechanical systems preferred.
